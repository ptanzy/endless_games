# Creator Ecosystem Principles

These principles define how the platform supports, empowers, and protects creators.

- Provide robust tools and resources for creators.
- Ensure fair monetization and recognition for creative work.
- Foster collaboration and community among creators.
- Protect creator rights and intellectual property.
- Enable easy sharing, discovery, and promotion of content.
- Support diverse creator backgrounds and styles.
- Maintain transparent policies for content moderation and monetization.
- Encourage innovation and experimentation.
- Adapt ecosystem features based on creator feedback.
