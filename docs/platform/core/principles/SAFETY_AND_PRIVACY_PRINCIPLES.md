# Safety and Privacy Principles

These principles ensure the platform is safe, secure, and respects user privacy at all times.

- Prioritize user safety in all platform features and policies.
- Protect personal data with strong security measures.
- Provide clear privacy controls and settings for users.
- Communicate privacy policies transparently and accessibly.
- Enable reporting and resolution of safety concerns quickly.
- Educate users about safety and privacy best practices.
- Adapt safety and privacy features to emerging threats and needs.
- Balance safety with user autonomy and freedom.
- Collaborate with experts and the community to improve safety.
