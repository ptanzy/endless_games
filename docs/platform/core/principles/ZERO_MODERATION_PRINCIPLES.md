# Zero Moderation Principles

These principles define the philosophy and rules for a platform that minimizes moderation, focusing on trust, transparency, and community-driven governance.

- Empower players to self-moderate and resolve disputes.
- Prioritize transparency in all moderation actions and decisions.
- Foster a culture of respect, inclusivity, and accountability.
- Use technology to prevent abuse while minimizing intervention.
- Ensure moderation tools are accessible and easy to use.
- Encourage positive behavior through incentives, not punishments.
- Maintain clear guidelines and communicate them openly.
- Protect vulnerable users without restricting freedom for others.
- Adapt moderation policies based on community feedback and evolving needs.
