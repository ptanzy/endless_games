# Balance Guidelines

This document provides guidelines for maintaining balance in chess gameplay and AI.# BALANCE GUIDELINES

## Overview
Provides guidelines for balancing gameplay across modes and rule modules.

---

## Key Balance Factors
- Board size  
- Player count  
- Movement rules  
- Promotion frequency  
- Elimination behavior  
- Terrain and portals  
- Endgame mutations  

---

## Balance Strategies
- Limit vertical mobility  
- Restrict portal frequency  
- Adjust pawn promotion difficulty  
- Modify king safety rules  
- Tune terrain hazard density  

---

## Testing Methodology
- Simulated matches  
- AI vs AI  
- Human playtesting  
- Statistical analysis  

---

## Notes
Balance is ongoing and must be revisited as new variants are added.
