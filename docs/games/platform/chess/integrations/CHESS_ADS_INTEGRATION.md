# CHESS — ADS INTEGRATION

## Purpose
This document defines how Chess integrates with the EndlessGames ad system. Chess follows strict platform rules: **no ads during gameplay**, **no forced ads**, **no pay‑to‑win**, and **all ads are optional**.

---

## Allowed Ad Surfaces (Optional)

### 1. Match Creation Screen
- Optional banner slot
- Optional rewarded ad button (“Earn EndlessCoin before match”)

### 2. Match Lobby
- Optional banner slot
- Optional rewarded ad button

### 3. Loading Screen
- Optional static brand frame (no video, no audio)

### 4. After‑Action Screen
- Optional rewarded ad button (“Earn bonus EndlessCoin”)

### 5. Profile & Clan Pages (Indirect)
- Chess Moments and Tube videos may generate ad impressions

---

## Forbidden Ad Surfaces
Chess **never** shows ads:

- During gameplay  
- During turn resolution  
- On the board  
- On pieces  
- In the HUD  
- In the 3D view  
- In competitive ranked modes  

---

## Reward Rules
Rewarded ads grant:

- EndlessCoin  
- Small XP bonuses  
- Optional event currency  

Never gameplay power.

---

## Ad‑to‑Cash Eligibility
Chess contributes indirectly via:

- EndlessTube impressions  
- EndlessTV impressions  
- Sponsored Moments  
- Brand playlists  

Chess itself does not show cash‑eligible ads.

---

## Membership Integration
Members may:

- Disable Chess ad surfaces  
- Receive +10% EndlessCoin from rewarded ads  
- Receive +5% cash bonus from eligible media ads
