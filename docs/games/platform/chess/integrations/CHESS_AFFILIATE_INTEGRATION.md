# CHESS — AFFILIATE INTEGRATION

## Purpose
This document defines how Chess interacts with EndlessDeals, surveys, and offerwalls. Chess does not directly host affiliate offers but contributes indirectly.

---

## Direct Integration
Chess does **not** display affiliate offers inside gameplay or UI.

---

## Indirect Integration

### 1. EndlessTube
Chess videos may generate:

- Affiliate impressions  
- Affiliate conversions  
- Creator revenue share  

### 2. EndlessTV
Chess highlights may appear in:

- Sponsored playlists  
- Brand blocks  
- Affiliate‑linked segments  

### 3. EndlessMoments
Chess Moments may appear in:

- Sponsored highlight reels  
- Brand‑themed events  

---

## Reward Rules
Affiliate rewards always grant:

- EndlessCoin  
- Optional event currency  

Never gameplay power.

---

## Membership Integration
Members receive:

- +10% EndlessCoin from affiliate rewards  
- Optional deal alerts in EndlessHub
