# CHESS — PROGRESSION & RANKING

## Purpose
This document defines how Chess integrates with EndlessRank, game‑specific rank, EndlessPass, Quests, and Events.

---

## Game‑Specific Rank
Chess has its own competitive ladder:

- MMR‑based  
- Seasonal resets  
- Rank badges  
- Rank‑based cosmetics  
- Rank‑based EndlessCoin rewards  

---

## EndlessRank Integration
Chess contributes to EndlessRank via:

- Wins  
- Performance  
- Achievements  
- Seasonal participation  
- Creator activity (Tube/Moments)  

---

## EndlessPass Integration
Chess contributes to EndlessPass XP through:

- Matches played  
- Wins  
- Daily/weekly quests  
- Seasonal events  
- Moments captured  

---

## EndlessQuests Integration
Chess supports:

- Daily quests  
- Weekly quests  
- Seasonal quests  
- Cross‑game quests  

Examples:
- “Win 3 matches in any mode”
- “Promote 5 pawns”
- “Capture 10 pieces”

---

## EndlessEvents Integration
Chess participates in:

- Seasonal events  
- Clan events  
- Brand‑sponsored events  
- Cross‑game mega events  

---

## Achievements
Chess achievements include:

- Mode‑specific achievements  
- Rule‑specific achievements  
- Skill achievements  
- Seasonal achievements
