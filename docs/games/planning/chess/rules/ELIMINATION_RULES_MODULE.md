# ELIMINATION RULES MODULE

## Overview
This module defines how player elimination behaves when a king is captured. These rules dramatically affect game flow and strategy.

---

## Elimination Options

### 1. Remove All Pieces
- When a king is captured, all of that player’s pieces are removed from the board.
- Simplest and cleanest option.

---

### 2. Conquest Mode
- When a king is captured, all of that player’s pieces transfer to the capturing player.
- Creates dramatic power swings.
- Works well in 4-player free-for-all.

---

### 3. Freeze Pieces (Variant)
- Eliminated player’s pieces remain on the board but cannot move.
- They still block movement and line-of-sight.

---

### 4. Neutral Pieces (Variant)
- Eliminated player’s pieces remain but become neutral.
- Any player may capture them.
- They do not block line-of-sight.

---

## King Capture Behavior
- King capture always results in immediate elimination.
- No resurrection mechanics unless added in future modules.

---

## Notes
Elimination rules interact with:
- Team rules  
- Movement rules  
- Endgame mutation rules
