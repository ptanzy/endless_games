# ADVANCED MECHANICS MODULE

## Overview
This module contains optional mechanics that significantly alter gameplay. These are high-impact toggles intended for variant play.

---

## 1. Decoration Rule (Capture → Gain Powers)
- When a piece captures another piece, it gains that piece’s movement abilities.
- Abilities may stack unless stacking is disabled.

---

## 2. Recruitment Rule (Captured Pieces Join You)
- Captured pieces change color and join the capturing player’s army.
- Works well with Conquest Mode.

---

## 3. Bare King Restrictions
- A king with no remaining pieces (“bare king”) is restricted from moving in the third dimension.
- Prevents runaway survival in 3D modes.

---

## 4. King Empowerment (Variant)
- Kings gain additional movement or capture abilities in late game.

---

## 5. Forced Aggression (Variant)
- If a capture is available, the player must capture.

---

## 6. Fog of War (Variant)
- Players only see squares they threaten or occupy.

---

## Notes
These mechanics are optional and intended for advanced or chaotic play.
