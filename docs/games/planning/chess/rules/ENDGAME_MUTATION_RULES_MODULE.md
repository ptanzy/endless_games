# ENDGAME MUTATION RULES MODULE

## Overview
Endgame Mutations are optional rule sets that activate after a time limit or turn threshold. They change the game’s dynamics to accelerate or intensify the endgame.

---

## Trigger Options
- After X minutes  
- After X turns  
- After X full rounds  

---

## Mutation Types

### 1. Sudden Death
- No draws allowed.
- First checkmate eliminates the player immediately.

---

### 2. Accelerated Promotion
- Pawns promote earlier or automatically after X turns.

---

### 3. Shrinking Board
- Outer ring becomes inactive, dangerous, or teleport-only.

---

### 4. Forced Aggression
- If a capture is available, it must be taken.
- Repetition is illegal.

---

### 5. Fog of War Activation
- Vision becomes limited to threatened or adjacent squares.

---

### 6. Portal Activation
- Portals appear or begin shifting.

---

### 7. King Empowerment
- Kings gain additional movement or capture abilities.

---

## Notes
Endgame mutations dramatically alter pacing and should be used intentionally.
