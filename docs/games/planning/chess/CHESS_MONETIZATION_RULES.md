# CHESS — MONETIZATION RULES

## Purpose
This document defines how monetization interacts with Chess on EndlessGames. Chess follows the platform’s strict player‑first, cosmetic‑only monetization philosophy.

---

## Core Principles
- **No pay‑to‑win**  
- **No gameplay advantage**  
- **No loot boxes**  
- **No forced ads**  
- **Cosmetic‑only purchases**  
- **Optional ads and offers**  
- **Unified cosmetics across all games**

---

## Cosmetic Surfaces in Chess
Chess supports the following cosmetic types:

- **Play model outfits** (visible in UI, not on the board)
- **Avatar frames**
- **Reaction packs**
- **Board themes**
- **Piece skins** (cosmetic only)
- **Layer skins** (Cube/Matrix)
- **Portal skins**
- **Terrain skins**
- **Victory animations**
- **Profile themes**

All cosmetics are platform‑wide and follow EndlessGames rules.

---

## EndlessCoin Usage
Players may spend EndlessCoin on:

- Board themes  
- Piece skins  
- Reaction packs  
- Profile themes  
- Seasonal cosmetics  
- Event cosmetics  

---

## EndlessGold Usage
Players may spend EndlessGold on:

- Premium cosmetics  
- Licensed IP cosmetics  
- Prestige cosmetics  
- Animated reactions  

---

## Ads & Offers
Chess supports **optional** monetization surfaces:

- Rewarded ads (opt‑in)
- EndlessTube/TV impressions (indirect)
- Offerwalls and surveys (indirect)
- Affiliate offers (indirect)

Chess **never** shows ads during gameplay.

---

## Membership Integration
Members receive:

- Cosmetic bonuses  
- EndlessCoin boosts  
- XP boosts  
- Ad control toggles  

Membership does **not** affect gameplay.
