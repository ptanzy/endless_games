# Creator System

## 1. Purpose
The Creator System empowers users to design, publish, and monetize content within the platform. It ensures:
- Safe, fair, and high-quality content creation
- Transparent monetization and attribution
- Protection against abuse and exploitation

---

## 2. Core Principles

### 2.1 Template-Driven Creation
- All content is built using approved templates and tools
- Arbitrary code or logic is not allowed

### 2.2 Attribution & Ownership
- All content is attributed to its creator(s)
- Ownership is transparent and auditable

### 2.3 Monetization Alignment
- Monetization is tied to content quality and engagement
- No zero-effort or exploitative monetization

### 2.4 Safety & Moderation
- All content is subject to automated and manual review
- Clear escalation and appeal processes exist

---

## 3. Content Types
- Games
- Cosmetics
- Events
- Media
- Social features

---

## 4. Creation Workflow
1. Select template
2. Configure content
3. Validate (automated checks)
4. Submit for review
5. Approve and publish

---

## 5. Monetization Rules
- Revenue shares are transparent and published
- Payouts are subject to anti-fraud and compliance checks
- Minimum quality and engagement thresholds for monetization

---

## 6. Anti-Exploit Mechanisms
- Automated detection of low-effort or spam content
- Rate limits on content creation and monetization
- Manual review triggers for suspicious activity

---

## 7. Observability
- Dashboards for content quality, engagement, and monetization
- Regular audits of creator activity and payouts

---

## 8. Open Decisions
- Template expansion policy
- Monetization formula tuning
- Creator appeal process

---

## 9. Summary
The Creator System ensures:
- All content is high-quality, safe, and fairly monetized
- Creators are empowered and protected
- The platform remains vibrant, innovative, and resilient to abuse
