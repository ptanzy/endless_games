# Economy & Reward System

## 1. Purpose

The Economy System defines:

- how value is generated  
- how rewards are distributed  
- how currency flows through the system  

This system ensures:

> Rewards are fair, sustainable, and directly tied to real revenue.

---

## 2. Core Principles

---

### 2.1 Revenue-Backed Rewards

- all rewards must originate from:
  - real revenue (ads, purchases)  

---

---

### 2.2 Effort-Based Distribution

- rewards scale with:
  - player effort  
  - validated gameplay  

---

---

### 2.3 Anti-Inflation

- total rewards must:
  - not exceed revenue-backed limits  

---

---

### 2.4 Controlled Liquidity

- higher-value currencies:
  - are scarce  
- lower-value currencies:
  - are abundant  

---

---
