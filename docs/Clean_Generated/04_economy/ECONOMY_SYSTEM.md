# Economy System

## 1. Purpose
The Economy System governs all value flows, currency management, and transaction rules across the platform. It ensures:
- Integrity of all economic activity
- Prevention of exploits and inflation
- Transparent, auditable value movement

---

## 2. Core Principles

### 2.1 Closed-Loop Economy
- All value must circulate within the platform
- No arbitrary value creation or destruction

### 2.2 Traceability
- Every transaction is logged and auditable
- Full value provenance is enforced

### 2.3 Anti-Exploit Design
- All economic actions are validated for exploit patterns
- Automated anomaly detection is required

### 2.4 Player Effort Alignment
- Rewards are always tied to meaningful player effort
- No passive or zero-effort value accrual

---

## 3. Currency Model
- Primary Currency: Platform Token (fungible)
- Secondary Currencies: Cosmetic Points, Event Tokens (optional, non-fungible)
- All currencies:
  - Have capped supply or controlled issuance
  - Are non-transferable outside the platform

---

## 4. Transaction Types
- Earn (from gameplay, creation, events)
- Spend (on cosmetics, access, upgrades)
- Transfer (between users, with strict limits)
- Burn (removal from circulation, e.g., for premium items)

All transactions:
- Must be atomic and reversible only via admin review
- Are subject to rate limits and anti-fraud checks

---

## 5. Reward System
- Rewards are distributed based on:
  - Player effort
  - Game/session outcomes
  - Event participation
- All reward formulas:
  - Are transparent and published
  - Have diminishing returns to prevent farming

---

## 6. Marketplace Rules
- All trades are mediated by the platform
- No direct peer-to-peer trading outside approved channels
- Price floors/ceilings enforced to prevent manipulation
- All listings and trades are logged

---

## 7. Anti-Exploit Mechanisms
- Automated detection of:
  - Rapid, repetitive transactions
  - Collusion patterns
  - Value laundering attempts
- Manual review triggers for flagged activity
- Dynamic adjustment of limits in response to detected exploits

---

## 8. Economic Observability
- Real-time dashboards for:
  - Currency supply
  - Transaction volumes
  - Reward distributions
  - Exploit indicators
- Regular audits and anomaly reports

---

## 9. Open Decisions
- Currency supply cap adjustment policy
- Marketplace expansion strategy
- Reward formula tuning cadence

---

## 10. Summary
The Economy System ensures:
- All value is earned, spent, and transferred in a controlled, transparent, and exploit-resistant manner
- The platform economy remains fair, sustainable, and aligned with player effort and engagement
