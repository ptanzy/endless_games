# Safety System

## Purpose
The Safety System protects users and the platform from harm, abuse, and exploitation. It ensures:
- Proactive detection and mitigation of threats
- User well-being and trust
- Compliance with legal and ethical standards

---

## Core Principles

### Proactive Protection
- Threats are detected and mitigated before harm occurs

### User Empowerment
- Users have tools to protect themselves (block, report, privacy controls)

### Transparency
- All safety actions are logged and reviewable

### Continuous Improvement
- Safety systems are regularly updated based on new threats and user feedback

---

## Threat Model
- Abuse (harassment, bullying)
- Exploits (cheating, fraud)
- Inappropriate content
- Privacy violations

---

## Protection Mechanisms
- Automated moderation (AI/ML, rules-based)
- Manual review workflows
- User reporting and escalation
- Rate limiting and anomaly detection

---

## User Controls
- Block/mute features
- Privacy settings (profile visibility, data sharing)
- Parental controls (for minors)

---

## Incident Response
- Real-time alerting for critical threats
- Triage and escalation protocols
- Post-incident review and remediation

---

## Observability
- Dashboards for safety incidents, response times, and resolution rates
- Regular safety audits and public transparency reports

---

## Open Decisions
- AI moderation tuning and transparency
- User appeal process for moderation actions
- Safety metric publication cadence

---

## Summary
The Safety System ensures:
- Users are protected from harm and abuse
- The platform is resilient to threats and adapts to new risks
- All safety actions are transparent, reviewable, and continuously improved
