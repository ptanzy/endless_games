# BRAND_MEDIA_PLANNING

---

## 🎯 Purpose & Philosophy

The Brand Media Framework defines how brands integrate into EndlessGames’ media ecosystem:

- EndlessTube (gameplay‑only VOD)
- EndlessTV (auto‑curated highlight feed)
- EndlessStream (future gameplay‑only streaming)
- EndlessRadio (future curated music channels)
- EndlessMoments (auto‑generated clips)
- EndlessRecaps (seasonal recap videos)

The philosophy is to ensure all brand media integrations are:

- Safe  
- Preset‑only  
- Gameplay‑only  
- Cosmetic‑only  
- Age‑appropriate  
- Zero‑moderation  
- Consistent with EndlessGames’ visual identity  

Brand media should enhance discovery, identity, and celebration without ever compromising player safety or gameplay fairness.

---

## 🧩 Problems This Solves

- Brands want visibility across media surfaces without unsafe user‑generated content.  
- Creators want brand‑sponsored visibility and monetization opportunities.  
- EndlessGames needs a unified, controlled system for brand media placements.  
- Players want curated, safe, gameplay‑only media experiences.  
- The platform needs scalable, repeatable media sponsorship formats.

The Brand Media Framework solves these by defining **clear rules**, **controlled surfaces**, and **platform‑mediated integrations**.

### 3. Media Review & Approval

All brand media must pass:

- Safety review  
- Age‑appropriateness review  
- Visual consistency review  
- Technical review  
- Brand tier alignment  

No external audio, video, or user‑generated assets are allowed.

---

### 4. Scheduling & Lifecycle

1. **Proposal**
   - Brand or internal team proposes media integration.

2. **Review**
   - Safety, feasibility, and alignment checks.

3. **Production**
   - Overlays, frames, playlists, and blocks created by EndlessGames.

4. **Scheduling**
   - Media programming team schedules blocks, playlists, or channels.

5. **Activation**
   - Media surfaces go live.

6. **Post‑Campaign**
   - Media removed or archived.
   - Analytics generated (future).

## 📏 Rules & Constraints

### Safety
- No user‑generated audio or video  
- No open text  
- No external uploads  
- All media must be gameplay‑only  

### Cosmetic‑Only
- No gameplay impact  
- No competitive advantage  

### Age‑Appropriate
- All brand media must pass age filters  
- No mature themes  

### Platform‑Mediated
- EndlessGames controls all media surfaces  
- Brands cannot directly upload media assets  

### Consistency
- All overlays and frames must match EndlessGames’ visual language

## ⚠️ Risks & Considerations

- Over‑branding may reduce player trust.  
- Media clutter may reduce clarity.  
- Need to maintain consistent visual identity.  
- Must avoid brand conflicts during seasonal events.  
- Must ensure all media remains gameplay‑only.
