# BRAND_AMBASSADOR_PLANNING

---

## 🎯 Purpose & Philosophy

The Brand Ambassador Marketplace enables **safe, preset‑only, cosmetic‑only sponsorships** between brands and:

- Players  
- Creators  
- Clans  

It is designed to:

- Empower creators and high‑performing players  
- Give brands a safe, controlled way to participate in the platform  
- Generate real‑world revenue for creators  
- Maintain EndlessGames’ zero‑moderation, zero‑toxicity foundation  
- Ensure all brand integrations remain cosmetic‑only and age‑appropriate  

This system is a **major revenue pillar** and a **core identity pillar** for the platform.

---

## 🧩 Problems This Solves

- Brands want to sponsor creators and players, but traditional platforms require moderation and open communication.  
- Players want ways to earn real value without unsafe interactions.  
- Creators want predictable, platform‑mediated brand deals.  
- Clans want identity, prestige, and cosmetic‑only sponsorship opportunities.  
- EndlessGames needs a scalable, safe, preset‑only brand integration layer.

The Brand Ambassador Marketplace solves all of these by **removing direct communication**, **centralizing approvals**, and **controlling all assets**.

## 📐 High‑Level Description

The Brand Ambassador Marketplace is a **platform‑mediated sponsorship system** where:

1. Brands submit sponsorship requests  
2. EndlessGames reviews and approves  
3. EndlessGames designs branded cosmetics  
4. Players/creators/clans opt in or out  
5. Branded swag appears across:
   - Play models  
   - Profiles  
   - Clan bases  
   - EndlessTube  
   - EndlessTV  
   - EndlessStream (future)  
6. Brands pay EndlessGames  
7. EndlessGames shares revenue with the sponsored user(s)

All interactions are **preset‑only**, **safe**, and **cosmetic‑only**.

## 🧵 Core System Components

### 1. Brand Request Flow
Brand selects:

- Player  
- Creator  
- Clan  
- Or a group (e.g., “top 10 creators in Match‑3”)  

Brand specifies:

- Campaign duration  
- Cosmetic theme  
- Surfaces (profile, play model, media, clan base)  
- Budget  
- Tier (if applicable)  

### 2. EndlessGames Review
- Safety review  
- Cosmetic feasibility  
- Age‑appropriateness  
- Brand tier alignment  
- Platform fit  

### 3. Swag Production Pipeline
Brand brief → concept → internal review → brand approval → implementation  

All swag is:

- Cosmetic‑only  
- Preset‑only  
- Safe for all ages  
- Visually aligned with EndlessGames  

### 4. Player/Creator/Clan Opt‑In
- Users receive a sponsorship offer  
- They can accept or decline  
- No penalties for declining  
- Acceptance triggers cosmetic assignment  

### 5. Revenue Share
Brands pay EndlessGames; EndlessGames pays:

- Creators (real‑world payouts)  
- Players (real‑world payouts for eligible tiers)  
- Clans (EndlessGold into clan treasury)  

### 6. Brand Visibility Surfaces
- Play model cosmetics  
- Profile banners  
- EndlessTube overlays  
- EndlessTV blocks  
- EndlessStream overlays (future)  
- Clan base decorations  
- Seasonal events  
- EndlessHub modules (future)

## 📏 Rules & Constraints

### Safety
- No direct brand ↔ player communication  
- No open text  
- No voice  
- No external assets  
- All cosmetics must be preset‑only  

### Cosmetic‑Only
- No gameplay advantage  
- No stat boosts  
- No competitive impact  

### Age‑Appropriate
- Brands must pass age‑group filters  
- Cosmetics must be safe for all ages  

### Platform‑Mediated
EndlessGames handles:

- Approvals  
- Payouts  
- Asset creation  
- Communication  
- Analytics
