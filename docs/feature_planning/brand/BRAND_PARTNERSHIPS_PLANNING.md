# Brand Partnerships Planning

This document details the structure and governance of brand partnerships, including onboarding, collaboration, and compliance.

---

## 🎯 Purpose
Define the framework for establishing, managing, and scaling brand partnerships with external companies, creators, organizations, and IP holders.

---

# 1. Goals
- Enable structured partnership creation
- Support co‑branded campaigns and assets
- Maintain legal and brand safety compliance
- Provide measurable performance outcomes

---

# 2. Personas
- **Brand Manager:** Manages partnerships
- **Partner:** Wants clear terms and easy collaboration
- **Legal:** Needs compliance and auditability

---

# 3. User Stories
- *As a partner, I want clear deliverables and timelines.*
- *As a brand manager, I want to track partnership performance.*

---

# 4. Core Capabilities
- Partnership tiers  
- Contract templates  
- Co‑branding guidelines  
- Campaign integration  
- Performance dashboards  

---

# 5. Workflows

### **5.1 Partnership Creation**
1. Outreach or inbound request  
2. Fit assessment  
3. Contracting  
4. Asset exchange  
5. Launch  

### **5.2 Partnership Management**
1. Track KPIs  
2. Review deliverables  
3. Renew or sunset  

---

# 6. Rules & Constraints
- No unapproved co‑branding  
- All partnerships must meet brand safety standards  
- Legal review required for all contracts  

---

# 7. Deep Dive
- Partnership categories  
- Revenue share models  
- Co‑branded asset pipeline  

---

# 8. Success Criteria
- High‑quality partnerships  
- Strong brand alignment  
- Positive ROI
