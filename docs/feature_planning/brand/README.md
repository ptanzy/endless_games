# Brand Planning README

---

## What Belongs Here
- Documents for brand strategy, partnerships, ambassador programs, brand asset management, and swag pipelines
- Planning for brand events, media integration, and brand-driven content

## What Doesn’t Belong Here
- Core platform rules, gameplay systems, or technical specs
- Cosmetic, creator, economy, media, social, or tools-specific planning

## Relationship to Other Folders
- Brand assets may flow into cosmetics, media, and creator content
- Brand partnerships can impact economy and events
- Brand safety and standards are informed by core doctrines
