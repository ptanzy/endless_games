# Prestige Cosmetics Planning

This document defines the prestige cosmetics system, including unlock criteria, exclusivity, and integration with progression systems.

---

## 🎯 Purpose
Define the Prestige Cosmetics system — ultra‑rare, long‑term progression cosmetics earned through mastery, loyalty, and seasonal achievements. Prestige cosmetics are never purchasable and represent the highest tier of cosmetic identity.

---

# 1. Goals
- Reward long‑term engagement  
- Provide exclusive, non‑purchasable cosmetics  
- Maintain fairness and transparency  
- Integrate with Prestige progression and seasonal systems  

---

# 2. Personas
- **Veteran Player:** Wants long‑term goals  
- **Collector:** Wants ultra‑rare cosmetics  
- **Platform:** Needs a fair, grind‑based prestige system  

---

# 3. User Stories
- *As a veteran player, I want cosmetics that show my mastery.*  
- *As a collector, I want ultra‑rare cosmetics that can’t be bought.*  
- *As the platform, I want prestige cosmetics to be fair and grind‑based.*  

---

# 4. Core Capabilities
- Prestige cosmetic unlocks  
- Seasonal prestige rewards  
- Prestige tiers and milestones  
- Cross‑game prestige identity  
- Integration with EndlessVault and EndlessPass  

---

# 5. Workflows

### **5.1 Prestige Unlock Workflow**
1. Player reaches prestige milestone  
2. System checks eligibility  
3. Prestige cosmetic unlocked  
4. Cosmetic added to inventory  

### **5.2 Seasonal Prestige Workflow**
1. Player completes seasonal mastery track  
2. Prestige cosmetic awarded  
3. Cosmetic flagged as seasonal‑exclusive  

---

# 6. Rules & Constraints
- Never purchasable  
- Never part of deals or bundles  
- No gameplay advantage  
- No UGC  
- No unsafe content  
- All prestige unlocks logged  

---

# 7. Deep Dive

### **7.1 Prestige Cosmetic Types**
- Prestige avatars  
- Prestige frames  
- Prestige titles  
- Prestige effects  
- Prestige animations  
- Prestige skins (game‑specific)  

### **7.2 Prestige Sources**
- Prestige level milestones  
- Seasonal mastery  
- Clan achievements  
- Multi‑season loyalty rewards  

### **7.3 Prestige Philosophy**
- Earned, not bought  
- Long‑term identity  
- High rarity  
- High status  
- Zero pay‑to‑win  

---

# 8. Success Criteria
- Prestige cosmetics feel meaningful and exclusive  
- No monetization pressure  
- Strong long‑term engagement  
- Clear prestige identity across all games
