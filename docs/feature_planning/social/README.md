# Social Planning README

---

## What Belongs Here
- Documents for social systems, clans, hub, player expressions, preset chat, and social economy
- Planning for social interaction, communication, and community features

## What Doesn’t Belong Here
- Core, brand, cosmetic, creator, economy, media, or tools-specific planning
- Implementation details or technical specs

## Relationship to Other Folders
- Social systems integrate with economy, media, and creator content
- Social safety and communication are governed by core doctrines and tools
- Social rewards and achievements may tie into progression and events
