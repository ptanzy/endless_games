# MEDIA_SYSTEMS_OVERVIEW

---

## 🎯 Purpose

Provide a unified overview of all EndlessGames media systems — EndlessTube, EndlessTV, EndlessStream, EndlessRadio, EndlessMoments, and EndlessRecaps — and define how they work together to create a safe, gameplay‑only entertainment ecosystem.

---

# 1. Goals

- Deliver a zero‑moderation media ecosystem  
- Support creators with safe, gameplay‑only tools  
- Increase platform engagement through passive and active media  
- Provide brand‑safe surfaces for sponsorships  
- Integrate media with gameplay, economy, and identity  

---

# 2. Personas

- **Player:** Watches, shares, and earns from gameplay‑only media  
- **Creator:** Uploads, streams, and monetizes gameplay content  
- **Brand Partner:** Sponsors playlists, blocks, or channels  
- **Platform:** Curates, stitches, and distributes media safely  

---

# 3. User Stories

- *As a player, I want to watch gameplay‑only videos and highlights.*  
- *As a creator, I want tools to upload, stream, and grow my audience.*  
- *As a viewer, I want passive entertainment through EndlessTV.*  
- *As a brand, I want safe sponsorship surfaces.*  
- *As a player, I want seasonal recaps of my achievements.*  

---

# 4. Core Capabilities

- Gameplay‑only media ingestion  
- Auto‑curation and highlight generation  
- Live streaming with preset‑only interaction  
- Curated music channels  
- Seasonal recap generation  
- Cross‑media monetization  
- Brand‑safe sponsorship surfaces  

---

# 5. Workflows

### **5.1 Media Flow Across Systems**
1. Player generates gameplay  
2. EndlessMoments auto‑captures highlights  
3. Player uploads long‑form to EndlessTube  
4. EndlessTV stitches highlights into 24/7 feed  
5. EndlessStream broadcasts live gameplay  
6. EndlessRecaps summarize seasonal activity  
7. EndlessRadio provides curated music with ad‑to‑cash rewards  

---

# 6. Rules & Constraints

- **Gameplay‑only:** No external audio, webcam, or commentary  
- **Preset‑only interaction:** Comments, reactions, chat  
- **Brand‑safe:** All media surfaces must be safe for all ages  
- **Zero moderation:** Systems must enforce safety automatically  
- **No user‑generated audio:** Applies to Tube, TV, Stream, Moments, Recaps  

---

# 7. Deep Dive

### **7.1 EndlessTube (VOD)**
- Gameplay‑only uploads  
- Preset comments  
- Creator analytics  
- Ad monetization  
- Feeds EndlessTV and Recaps  

### **7.2 EndlessTV (24/7 Highlights)**
- Auto‑curated highlight feed  
- Seasonal playlists  
- Creator spotlights  
- Sponsored blocks  

### **7.3 EndlessStream (Live Streaming)**
- Gameplay‑only live streams  
- Preset reactions  
- Auto‑ads when offline  
- Partner Program for creators  

### **7.4 EndlessRadio (Music Channels)**
- Curated music catalog  
- Sponsored channels  
- Ad‑to‑cash rewards  
- Optional EndlessCoin/Gold license slots  

### **7.5 EndlessMoments (Auto‑Highlights)**
- Auto‑generated clips  
- Triggered by kills, wins, milestones  
- Feeds Tube and TV  
- Brand‑safe overlays  

### **7.6 EndlessRecaps (Seasonal Videos)**
- Auto‑generated seasonal summaries  
- Stats + highlights + achievements  
- Sponsored recap seasons  

---

# 8. Success Criteria

- All media remains gameplay‑only and safe  
- Creators can grow and monetize without moderation risk  
- Brands trust the platform for sponsorships  
- Players engage with media across all surfaces  
- Media strengthens identity, retention, and progression
