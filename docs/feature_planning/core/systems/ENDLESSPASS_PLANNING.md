# EndlessPass Planning

This document outlines the design and progression of the EndlessPass system, including reward structures, seasonality, and integration with other platform systems.

---

## 🎯 Purpose
Define the EndlessPass seasonal progression system, offering cosmetic rewards, currency, and prestige bonuses through a predictable, player-first seasonal track.

---

# 1. Goals
- Provide long-term seasonal progression
- Reward consistent engagement
- Integrate with events, quests, and cosmetics
- Avoid FOMO and pressure loops
- Support free and premium tracks

---

# 2. Personas
- **Player:** Wants steady rewards
- **Collector:** Wants seasonal cosmetics
- **Platform:** Needs predictable seasonal engagement

---

# 3. User Stories
- *As a player, I want a seasonal track that rewards my time.*
- *As a collector, I want unique seasonal cosmetics.*

---

# 4. Core Capabilities
- Seasonal XP
- Free and premium tracks
- Reward tiers
- Seasonal challenges
- Season timeline

---

# 5. Workflows

### **5.1 Season Start**
1. Season published  
2. Rewards revealed  
3. Challenges activated  

### **5.2 Player Progression**
1. Player earns Seasonal XP  
2. Unlocks tiers  
3. Claims rewards  

---

# 6. Rules & Constraints
- No pay-to-win  
- Premium track must be cosmetic-only  
- Seasonal XP must be earnable through normal play  

---

# 7. Deep Dive
- Seasonal XP sources  
- Reward pacing  
- Premium value philosophy  

---

# 8. Success Criteria
- High completion rate  
- Strong cosmetic engagement
