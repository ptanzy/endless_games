# PROGRESSION_SYSTEM_PLANNING

---

## 🎯 Purpose
Define the unified progression system for EndlessGames, including XP, leveling, perks, and prestige. This system provides a multi‑year progression arc, meaningful cosmetic‑only rewards, and a round‑robin perk system that grows evenly across all 100 levels.

---

# 1. Goals
- Provide a ~5‑year progression arc to Level 100  
- Reward engagement across games, media, and social systems  
- Deliver meaningful, non‑power perks every level  
- Maintain zero‑moderation, zero‑toxicity, and cosmetic‑only doctrine  
- Integrate with EndlessRank, EndlessPass, Events, Clans, and Creator systems  
- Support infinite Prestige without power creep  

---

# 2. Personas
- **Long‑Term Player:** Wants a multi‑year journey with meaningful milestones  
- **Optimizer:** Wants perks that improve earnings and discounts  
- **Cosmetic Collector:** Wants prestige visuals  
- **Competitive Player:** Wants to reach Level 100 first  
- **Platform:** Needs stable pacing and safe, sustainable progression  

---

# 3. User Stories
- *As a player, I want leveling to feel rewarding at every step.*  
- *As a player, I want perks that enhance my experience without affecting gameplay.*  
- *As a prestige player, I want cosmetic rewards that show my dedication.*  
- *As the platform, I need a progression system that avoids power creep.*  

---

# 4. Core Capabilities
- XP accumulation  
- Level thresholds  
- Perk point assignment  
- Round‑robin perk progression  
- Prestige levels  
- Prestige cosmetic rewards  

---

# 5. XP System

## 5.1 XP Curve
- Non‑linear  
- Fast early levels, slower late levels  
- Total XP target for Level 100: **10–20 million XP**  
- Designed for ~5 years of steady play  

## 5.2 XP Sources
Players earn XP from:  
- Daily quests  
- Weekly quests  
- Achievements  
- Seasonal events  
- Gameplay across all games  
- EndlessTube engagement  
- EndlessRadio listening  
- Offerwalls and surveys  
- Clan events  
- Creator milestones  

---

# 6. Leveling System

## 6.1 Level Range
- Levels 1–100  
- Each level awards **1 Perk Point**  
- Total Perk Points: **100**  

## 6.2 Level‑Up Workflow
1. Player earns XP  
2. XP reaches threshold  
3. Level increases  
4. Perk Point awarded  
5. Perk auto‑assigned via round‑robin system  

---

# 7. Perk System (Round‑Robin Model)

## 7.1 Overview
The Perk System provides **10 perks**, each with **10 levels**, for a total of **100 perk upgrades**.  
Players earn **1 perk level per player level**, and perks advance in a **round‑robin sequence**:



Level 1  → Perk 1 (Level 1)
Level 2  → Perk 2 (Level 1)
...
Level 10 → Perk 10 (Level 1)

Level 11 → Perk 1 (Level 2)
Level 12 → Perk 2 (Level 2)
...
Level 20 → Perk 10 (Level 2)

...
Level 100 → Perk 10 (Level 10)


This ensures:
- Every perk grows evenly  
- No perk is front‑loaded or back‑loaded  
- Every level feels meaningful  
- Balancing is dramatically simpler  

---

## 7.2 The 10 Perks (Finalized Order)

### **1. XP Boost**  
Increases XP earned from all sources.

### **2. EndlessCoin Boost**  
Increases EndlessCoin earned from gameplay, media, and events.

### **3. Ad Reward Boost**  
Increases EndlessCoin earned from rewarded ads and media impressions.

### **4. Event Reward Boost**  
Increases rewards from seasonal events and quests.

### **5. EndlessPass Boost**  
Increases EndlessPass XP or milestone rewards.

### **6. Clan Contribution Boost**  
Increases clan event points or clan treasury contributions.

### **7. Creator Support Boost**  
Increases EndlessCoin earned from creator milestones, Tube uploads, or Moments usage.

### **8. Membership Discount Boost**  
Provides a percentage discount on monthly or quarterly memberships.

### **9. Ad‑to‑Cash Boost**  
Increases the percentage of cash earned from eligible ad types.

### **10. Yearly Subscription Discount Boost**  
Provides a percentage discount on yearly membership.  
**Birthday month discount is always rolled into yearly pricing.**  
Example:  
- Monthly = $10  
- Yearly = $96  
- Birthday month bonus = −$8  
- Final yearly = $88  
Perk discounts stack additively (non‑compounding).

---

## 7.3 Perk Rules & Constraints
- No gameplay advantages  
- No stat boosts  
- No competitive impact  
- No perk stacking exploits  
- All perks must remain economically safe  
- All perks must reinforce player‑first philosophy  

---

# 8. Prestige System

## 8.1 Overview
Prestige unlocks at Level 100 and provides infinite cosmetic‑only progression.

## 8.2 Prestige Rules
- Prestige Levels are infinite  
- No additional Perk Points  
- No gameplay advantages  
- XP continues accumulating  

## 8.3 Prestige Rewards
- Prestige borders  
- Prestige auras  
- Prestige particle effects  
- Prestige badges  
- Prestige profile themes  
- Seasonal prestige cosmetics  

## 8.4 First‑Player Legacy System
The first player to reach Level 100 in a season/year selects from preset options:  
- Accent color  
- Emblem  
- Particle style  
- Aura animation  

This selection influences the next season’s Prestige cosmetic set.  
No user‑generated content.
