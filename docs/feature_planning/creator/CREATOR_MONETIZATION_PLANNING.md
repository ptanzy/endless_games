# CREATOR_MONETIZATION_PLANNING

---

## 🎯 Purpose

Define the systems that allow creators to earn EndlessCoin, EndlessGold, affiliate revenue, and real‑world payouts through safe, gameplay‑only content.

---

# 1. Goals

- Provide a safe, transparent monetization system  
- Reward creators for gameplay‑only content  
- Support ads, affiliate, and brand revenue  
- Enable real‑world payouts for Partner creators  
- Maintain Zero Moderation safety  

---

# 2. Personas

- **Entry Creator:** Wants to grow and unlock monetization  
- **Partner Creator:** Wants reliable revenue and brand deals  
- **Viewer:** Wants to support creators safely  
- **Brand Partner:** Wants to sponsor creators  

---

# 3. User Stories

- *As a creator, I want to earn tokens from my EndlessTube views.*  
- *As a creator, I want to unlock monetization as I grow.*  
- *As a Partner creator, I want real‑world payouts.*  
- *As a viewer, I want to support creators through preset‑safe interactions.*  
- *As a brand, I want to sponsor creators safely.*  

---

# 4. Core Capabilities

- Token earnings from views  
- Affiliate revenue share  
- Brand sponsorship payouts  
- Creator tiers  
- Creator analytics  
- Payout dashboards  

---

# 5. Workflows

### **5.1 Token Earnings**
1. Video receives views  
2. System calculates impressions  
3. Creator receives EndlessCoin  

### **5.2 Affiliate Revenue**
1. Viewer completes offer  
2. Affiliate partner pays EndlessGames  
3. Creator receives share  

### **5.3 Brand Deals**
1. Brand selects creator  
2. EndlessGames manages contract  
3. Creator receives payout  

---

# 6. Rules & Constraints

- No open communication between brands and creators  
- All payouts handled by EndlessGames  
- Gameplay‑only content  
- No external audio  
- No unsafe metadata  

---

# 7. Deep Dive

### **7.1 Creator Tiers**
- Entry → Mid → Partner  
- Unlock analytics, monetization, brand deals  

### **7.2 Revenue Streams**
- EndlessTube ads  
- EndlessTV ads  
- Affiliate offers  
- Brand sponsorships  
- Creator Tip Jar (future)  

### **7.3 Payout Rules**
- Quarterly payouts  
- USD or stablecoins  
- Minimum thresholds  

---

# 8. Success Criteria

- Creators earn safely  
- Monetization is transparent  
- No unsafe content enters the system
