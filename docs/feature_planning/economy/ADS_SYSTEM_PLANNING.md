# ADS_SYSTEM_PLANNING

---

## 🎯 Purpose

Define the deterministic, player‑first advertising system that powers EndlessCoin earnings, creator monetization, and brand integrations — while maintaining a safe, zero‑moderation environment.

---

# 1. Goals

- Provide optional, non‑intrusive ads  
- Reward players fairly for ad engagement  
- Support creator monetization  
- Enable brand partnerships  
- Maintain gameplay‑only and preset‑only safety rules  

---

# 2. Personas

- **Player:** Wants optional ads for rewards  
- **Creator:** Wants ad revenue from EndlessTube/TV  
- **Brand Partner:** Wants safe, high‑quality ad inventory  
- **Platform Operator:** Needs deterministic ad rules  

---

# 3. User Stories

- *As a player, I want to watch rewarded ads for EndlessCoin.*  
- *As a creator, I want ad revenue from my videos.*  
- *As a brand, I want safe ad placements with no user‑generated content risk.*  
- *As a clan, I want to enable optional ads to earn EndlessGold for the clan treasury.*  

---

# 4. Core Capabilities

- Rewarded ads  
- Auto‑ads (EndlessTV, EndlessStream offline mode)  
- Profile ads (optional)  
- Clan page ads (optional)  
- Brand‑sponsored blocks  
- Ad frequency controls  
- Ad safety validation  

---

# 5. Workflows

### **5.1 Rewarded Ads**
1. Player opts in  
2. Ad plays  
3. Impression logged  
4. Player receives EndlessCoin  

### **5.2 Creator Ad Revenue**
1. Viewer watches EndlessTube/TV  
2. Ad plays  
3. Creator receives share  

### **5.3 Brand Blocks**
1. Brand submits campaign  
2. EndlessGames approves  
3. Ads appear in curated blocks  

---

# 6. Rules & Constraints

- Ads never interrupt gameplay  
- Ads never autoplay with sound  
- Ads never appear in unsafe contexts  
- No ads in children’s profiles unless COPPA‑compliant  
- No manipulative timers or forced ads  
- All ads must be brand‑safe  

---

# 7. Deep Dive

### **7.1 Ad Surfaces**
- EndlessTube  
- EndlessTV  
- EndlessStream (offline)  
- EndlessRadio  
- Profile pages (optional)  
- Clan pages (optional)  

### **7.2 Player‑Placed Ads**
- Players may opt into extra ads  
- Earn EndlessCoin or EndlessGold  
- No cash payouts  

### **7.3 Clan‑Placed Ads**
- Clans may enable ads  
- Earn EndlessGold into clan treasury  

---

# 8. Success Criteria

- Ads are optional and non‑intrusive  
- Players feel rewarded, not exploited  
- Creators earn fairly  
- Brands trust the platform
