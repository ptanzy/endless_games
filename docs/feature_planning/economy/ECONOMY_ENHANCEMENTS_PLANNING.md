# ECONOMY_ENHANCEMENTS_PLANNING

---

## 🎯 Purpose

Define future and far‑future enhancements to the EndlessGames economy, including gifting, wishlists, price alerts, tip jars, brand loyalty, and more.

---

# 1. Goals

- Expand generosity and social value  
- Improve long‑term retention  
- Support creators and brands  
- Maintain cosmetic‑only rules  

---

# 2. Personas

- **Player:** Wants more ways to earn and give  
- **Creator:** Wants more support from fans  
- **Brand Partner:** Wants loyalty systems  
- **Clan Leader:** Wants clan‑based rewards  

---

# 3. User Stories

- *As a player, I want to gift cosmetics to friends.*  
- *As a player, I want to wishlist items.*  
- *As a creator, I want a tip jar.*  
- *As a brand, I want loyalty tracks.*  

---

# 4. Core Capabilities

- Gifting  
- Wishlists  
- Price alerts  
- Creator tip jar  
- Brand loyalty tracks  
- Seasonal bonuses  

---

# 5. Workflows

### **5.1 Gifting**
1. Player selects item  
2. Selects friend  
3. Gift delivered  

### **5.2 Wishlist**
1. Player marks item  
2. System tracks price  
3. Player notified  

---

# 6. Rules & Constraints

- Cosmetic‑only  
- No gambling  
- No pay‑to‑win  
- No unsafe social interactions  

---

# 7. Deep Dive

### **7.1 Gifting**
- EndlessCoin or EndlessGold  
- Seasonal gifting events  

### **7.2 Wishlists**
- Price drops  
- Seasonal sales  
- Brand promotions  

### **7.3 Tip Jar**
- EndlessCoin or EndlessGold  
- No cash payouts  

### **7.4 Brand Loyalty**
- Earn cosmetics  
- Earn badges  
- Seasonal brand tracks  

---

# 8. Success Criteria

- Players feel generous  
- Creators feel supported  
- Brands gain long‑term engagement
