# EndlessCoin System Planning

This document outlines the design, rules, and governance for the EndlessCoin currency system. Define how EndlessCoin is earned, spent, managed, and protected to ensure a fair and secure economy.

---

## 🎯 Purpose
Define EndlessCoin as the primary soft‑currency of the EndlessGames ecosystem, earned through gameplay, ads, affiliate actions, and platform engagement. EndlessCoin fuels the player‑first economy by enabling generous rewards, fair progression, and cosmetic‑only purchases.

---

# 1. Goals
- Provide a stable, generous soft‑currency  
- Reward players for engagement, not spending  
- Support all earn surfaces (ads, affiliate, gameplay, social)  
- Maintain inflation control and long‑term value  
- Integrate with EndlessVault, EndlessDeals, and EndlessPass  

---

# 2. Personas
- **Player:** Wants meaningful, fair rewards  
- **Free Player:** Relies on EndlessCoin for progression  
- **Platform:** Needs stable, predictable currency flows  

---

# 3. User Stories
- *As a player, I want to earn EndlessCoin from normal play.*  
- *As a free player, I want to unlock cosmetics without paying.*  
- *As the platform, I want to prevent inflation and abuse.*  

---

# 4. Core Capabilities
- Earn EndlessCoin  
- Spend EndlessCoin  
- Daily/weekly earn caps  
- Anti‑abuse detection  
- Integration with ads, affiliate, and social systems  
- Vault multipliers and boosts  

---

# 5. Workflows

### **5.1 Earn Workflow**
1. Player completes action  
2. System checks earn rules  
3. Earn amount calculated  
4. Anti‑abuse filters applied  
5. EndlessCoin added to wallet  

### **5.2 Spend Workflow**
1. Player selects cosmetic or deal  
2. System checks balance  
3. Purchase validated  
4. EndlessCoin deducted  
5. Item delivered  

---

# 6. Rules & Constraints
- No pay‑to‑win uses  
- No gambling mechanics  
- Earn caps prevent farming  
- All earn sources must be transparent  
- All transactions logged  

---

# 7. Deep Dive

### **7.1 Earn Sources**
- Ads  
- Affiliate actions  
- Social engagement  
- Clan participation  
- EndlessPass milestones  
- Seasonal events  

### **7.2 Spend Sinks**
- Cosmetics  
- EndlessDeals  
- Vault deposits  
- Seasonal shop items  

### **7.3 Inflation Controls**
- Daily earn caps  
- Dynamic multipliers  
- Seasonal adjustments  
- Anti‑bot detection  

---

# 8. Success Criteria
- Players feel rewarded  
- Free players can fully participate  
- Economy remains stable  
- No inflation or abuse
