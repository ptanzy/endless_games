# EndlessGold System Planning

This document details the EndlessGold premium currency system, including acquisition, spending, conversion, and safeguards to maintain value and prevent exploitation.

---

## 🎯 Purpose
Define EndlessGold as the premium currency of EndlessGames, used for optional cosmetic purchases, premium bundles, and membership perks — always cosmetic‑only, never pay‑to‑win.

---

# 1. Goals
- Provide a premium cosmetic currency  
- Maintain fairness and transparency  
- Support optional purchases without pressure  
- Integrate with EndlessDeals, EndlessPass, and Membership  

---

# 2. Personas
- **Player:** Wants optional premium cosmetics  
- **Collector:** Wants exclusive items  
- **Platform:** Needs stable premium currency flows  

---

# 3. User Stories
- *As a player, I want premium cosmetics that feel worth it.*  
- *As a collector, I want exclusive items that don’t affect gameplay.*  
- *As the platform, I want predictable premium currency behavior.*  

---

# 4. Core Capabilities
- Purchase EndlessGold  
- Earn small amounts through events  
- Spend EndlessGold on cosmetics  
- Membership bonuses  
- Seasonal bundles  

---

# 5. Workflows

### **5.1 Purchase Workflow**
1. Player selects EndlessGold pack  
2. Payment processed  
3. EndlessGold delivered  
4. Transaction logged  

### **5.2 Spend Workflow**
1. Player selects cosmetic  
2. System checks balance  
3. Purchase validated  
4. Item delivered  

---

# 6. Rules & Constraints
- No gameplay advantage  
- No loot boxes  
- No gambling mechanics  
- All purchases reversible (within policy)  
- All transactions logged  

---

# 7. Deep Dive

### **7.1 Earn Sources**
- Seasonal events  
- Membership bonuses  
- Prestige rewards  

### **7.2 Spend Sinks**
- Premium cosmetics  
- Seasonal bundles  
- Prestige cosmetics  
- Vault boosts  

### **7.3 Pricing Philosophy**
- Fair  
- Transparent  
- Cosmetic‑only  
- No pressure mechanics  

---

# 8. Success Criteria
- Players feel EndlessGold is optional but valuable  
- No pay‑to‑win perception  
- Strong cosmetic engagement  
- Predictable premium economy
