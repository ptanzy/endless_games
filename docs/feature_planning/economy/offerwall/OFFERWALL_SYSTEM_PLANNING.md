# OFFERWALL_SYSTEM_PLANNING

---

## 🎯 Purpose

Define the offerwall system that rewards players for completing surveys, installs, and partner tasks.

---

# 1. Goals

- Provide safe, transparent offerwalls  
- Reward players fairly  
- Support affiliate partners  
- Maintain strict safety rules  

---

# 2. Personas

- **Player:** Wants EndlessCoin  
- **Partner:** Wants conversions  
- **Creator:** Wants affiliate share  

---

# 3. User Stories

- *As a player, I want clear time/value estimates.*  
- *As a player, I want to avoid predatory offers.*  
- *As a creator, I want affiliate revenue.*  

---

# 4. Core Capabilities

- Survey offers  
- Install offers  
- Purchase offers  
- Time‑based tasks  
- Reward distribution  

---

# 5. Workflows

### **5.1 Offer Completion**
1. Player selects offer  
2. Completes task  
3. Partner confirms  
4. Player rewarded  

---

# 6. Rules & Constraints

- No gambling  
- No adult content  
- No crypto scams  
- Clear disclosures  

---

# 7. Deep Dive

### **7.1 Offer Categories**
- Surveys  
- App installs  
- Purchases  
- Trials  
- Time tasks  

### **7.2 Safety**
- Manual review  
- Automated scanning  
- Partner vetting  

---

# 8. Success Criteria

- Players trust the offerwall  
- Partners see conversions  
- No unsafe offers slip through
