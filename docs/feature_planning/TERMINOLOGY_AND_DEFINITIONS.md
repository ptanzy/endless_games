# TERMINOLOGY_AND_DEFINITIONS

---

## 🎯 Purpose
Provide canonical definitions for key EndlessGames terms to prevent naming drift across planning docs.

---

## 🗣 Communication

**Preset‑Only:**  
All player‑facing communication uses predefined options.

**Preset Pack:**  
A purchasable or unlockable set of preset messages, reactions, or statuses.

---

## 👥 Social & Identity

**Profile:**  
Hybrid public page + private dashboard for a player.

**Clan:**  
Preset‑only social group with shared identity and events.

**Clan Base / Clan Hall:**  
Cosmetic‑only shared space for clans.

**Clan Treasury:**  
Shared EndlessGold balance earned via clan ads (no cash).

---

## 🎨 Cosmetics

**Cosmetic:**  
Any visual or expressive item with zero gameplay impact.

**Brand Swag:**  
Cosmetics created via brand partnerships or ambassador deals.

**Mini‑Swag:**  
Small accessories ideal for brand collabs.

---

## 💰 Economy

**EndlessCoin:**  
Earned currency used for most progression and cosmetics.

**EndlessGold:**  
Premium currency used for premium/licensed cosmetics and some perks.

**Ad‑to‑Cash:**  
Small percentage of ad rewards converted to gift‑card‑style cash.

---

## 📹 Media

**EndlessTube:**  
Gameplay‑only VOD platform.

**EndlessTV:**  
Auto‑curated 24/7 highlight feed.

**EndlessStream:**  
Gameplay‑only live streaming (future).

**EndlessRadio:**  
Curated music channels with controlled catalog.

**EndlessMoments:**  
Auto‑generated gameplay highlight clips.

**EndlessRecaps:**  
Seasonal recap videos summarizing player activity.

---

## 🧱 Progression

**EndlessRank:**  
Platform‑wide rank based on gameplay, achievements, creator activity, and engagement.

**Game‑Specific Rank:**  
Per‑game skill ladder.

**EndlessPass:**  
Seasonal progression track across games and media.

**Prestige:**  
Post‑cap, cosmetic‑only long‑term progression layer.
