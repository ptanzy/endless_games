# AI_PERSONALIZATION_PLANNING

---

## 🎯 Purpose
Define the AI‑driven personalization systems that tailor discovery, recommendations, and platform surfaces while maintaining zero‑moderation safety.

---

# 1. Goals
- Personalize player experience safely  
- Improve discovery across games and media  
- Support creator growth  
- Maintain preset‑only, gameplay‑only rules  

---

# 2. Personas
- **Player:** Wants relevant content and recommendations  
- **Creator:** Wants personalized exposure  
- **Platform:** Wants higher engagement without unsafe data  

---

# 3. User Stories
- *As a player, I want personalized game and media recommendations.*  
- *As a creator, I want my content shown to the right audience.*  
- *As the platform, I want personalization without unsafe data collection.*  

---

# 4. Core Capabilities
- Personalized EndlessHub feed  
- Personalized EndlessTube recommendations  
- Personalized event suggestions  
- Personalized store and cosmetic suggestions  

---

# 5. Workflows

### **5.1 Personalized Feed Workflow**
1. Player interacts with games/media  
2. System logs safe behavioral signals  
3. AI ranks relevant content  
4. Personalized feed updates  

---

# 6. Rules & Constraints
- No personal data sharing  
- No open text or unsafe signals  
- Only gameplay‑safe behavioral data  
- No external tracking  

---

# 7. Deep Dive

### **7.1 Personalization Inputs**
- Gameplay history  
- Creator follows  
- Clan membership  
- Seasonal events  
- EndlessTube watch history  
- EndlessRadio listening  

### **7.2 Personalization Surfaces**
- EndlessHub  
- EndlessTube  
- Store  
- Events  
- Cosmetics  

---

# 8. Success Criteria
- Feeds feel relevant  
- Creators grow faster  
- No moderation required  
- No unsafe personalization data
