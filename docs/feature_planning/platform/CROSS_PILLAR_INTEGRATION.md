# CROSS_PILLAR_INTEGRATION

---

## 🎯 Purpose
Define how major platform pillars integrate with one another to create a unified EndlessGames experience.

---

# 1. Pillars
- Identity  
- Social  
- Discovery  
- Creator  
- Seasonal  
- Economy  
- Media  
- Gameplay  

---

# 2. Integration Rules
- All pillars must integrate through defined interfaces  
- No pillar may bypass safety or identity rules  
- All integrations must be observable  
- All integrations must be reversible  

---

# 3. Key Integrations

### Identity ↔ Social
- Identity surfaces appear in social interactions  
- Presets reflect identity themes  

### Identity ↔ Creator
- Creator identity surfaces appear on content  
- Creator milestones unlock identity rewards  

### Creator ↔ Discovery
- Creator content appears in Hub modules  
- Creator tiers influence ranking  

### Seasonal ↔ All Pillars
- Seasonal identity overlays  
- Seasonal quests  
- Seasonal creator events  
- Seasonal clan competitions  

### Media ↔ Discovery
- Media content drives Hub engagement  
- Clips feed into Moments  

---

# 4. Anti‑Patterns
- Hard‑coded integrations  
- Cross‑pillar data writes  
- Unbounded dependencies
