# CREATOR_ECOSYSTEM_PLANNING

---

## 🎯 Purpose
Define the unified Creator Ecosystem for EndlessGames — including creator identity, creator tools, creator content surfaces, creator monetization, creator progression, and the integration of the EndlessCreator SDK (Content‑Extension, Game SDK, and Mini‑Game SDK).

---

# 1. Goals
- Empower creators to produce media, interactive content, and games  
- Maintain zero‑moderation safety  
- Support creator monetization  
- Strengthen creator visibility across Hub, media, and identity surfaces  
- Provide creators with a clear progression path  
- Integrate creators into clans, seasons, and brand partnerships  

---

# 2. Personas

### **Creator**
Produces videos, streams, interactive content, games, or modes.

### **Viewer / Player**
Watches, plays, and interacts safely with creator content.

### **Platform**
Ensures safety, consistency, and scalable creator tools.

### **Brand Partner**
Collaborates with creators through preset‑safe integrations.

---

# 3. User Stories

- *As a creator, I want tools to publish videos, streams, and interactive content.*  
- *As a creator, I want to build games using the EndlessCreator SDK.*  
- *As a creator, I want preset‑safe interactions with my audience.*  
- *As a creator, I want analytics that help me grow.*  
- *As a creator, I want to monetize my content safely.*  
- *As a viewer, I want to follow creators and see their content in my Hub.*  
- *As the platform, I want creator systems that require no moderation.*  

---

- Creator identity surfaces

---

# 5. Workflows

## **5.1 Creator Media Publishing**
1. Creator uploads or records content  
2. System validates safety  
3. Content appears on Hub + creator profile  

---

## **5.2 Creator SDK Publishing**
1. Creator builds experience using SDK  
2. System validates safety  
3. Experience reviewed  
4. Experience published  

---

## **5.3 Creator Interaction Workflow**
1. Viewer selects preset comment or reaction  
2. Interaction appears instantly  
3. Creator receives safe engagement signals  

---

## **5.4 Creator Monetization Workflow**
1. Creator publishes eligible content or games  
2. Platform tracks engagement  
3. Revenue share calculated  
4. Creator receives payout  

---

# 6. Rules & Constraints

- No open text  
- No personal data  
- No unsafe communication  
- All content must be gameplay‑only  
- All assets must be reviewed  
- All SDK outputs must follow platform safety rules  
- Monetization is cosmetic‑only

# 7. Deep Dive

---

## **7.1 Creator Identity**

Creators have:

- Creator profile

## **7.2 Creator Content Types**

Creators can publish:

- EndlessTube videos  
- EndlessTV highlights  
- EndlessStream sessions  
- EndlessMoments clips  
- Interactive content (SDK v1)  
- Full games (SDK v2)  
- Mini‑modes (SDK v3)  

---

## **7.3 Creator SDK Integration**

Creators progress through three SDK tiers:

### **Tier 1 — Content‑Extension SDK**
Interactive stories, puzzles, challenges, branching clips.

### **Tier 2 — Game SDK**
Full creator‑built games using approved components.

### **Tier 3 — Mini‑Game / Mode SDK**
Modes inside specific EndlessGames titles.

All SDK outputs integrate with:

- Hub  
- Player Expression  
- Preset Communication  
- Clans  
- Seasonal Identity  
- Brand Integrations
