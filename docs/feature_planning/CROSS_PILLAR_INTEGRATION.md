# CROSS_PILLAR_INTEGRATION

---

## 🎯 Purpose
Describe how the major pillars (core, brand, cosmetics, creator, economy, media, social, tools, future) interact and depend on each other.

---

## 🧱 Pillars

- **Core:** identity, safety, progression, ranks, achievements
- **Brand:** partnerships, ambassadors, swag, brand media
- **Cosmetics:** cosmetic system, layers, rules, pipelines
- **Creator:** creator tools, monetization, SDK
- **Economy:** currencies, ads, affiliate, membership, token rules
- **Media:** Tube, TV, Stream, Radio, Moments, Recaps
- **Social:** profiles, clans, preset communication, identity surfaces
- **Tools:** observability, performance, release, testing, internal dashboards
- **Future:** far‑future systems and experiments

---

## 🔗 Key Cross‑Pillar Flows

### Core ↔ Everything
- Identity, ranks, achievements, and progression are upstream of most systems.

### Brand ↔ Cosmetics ↔ Media
- Brand swag flows through the cosmetic system.
- Brand media integrations appear on Tube, TV, Radio, Stream, Moments, Recaps.

### Brand ↔ Social ↔ Economy
- Brand‑sponsored clan tournaments and clan swag.
- Clan ads generate EndlessGold into clan treasuries.

### Creator ↔ Media ↔ Economy
- Creator content lives in Tube/TV/Stream.
- Ads, affiliate, and brand deals generate tokens and real‑world payouts.

### Social ↔ Core ↔ Media
- Profiles and clans surface ranks, achievements, media, and cosmetics.

### Tools ↔ All Pillars
- Observability, performance, release, and testing support every system.

---

## 📏 Integration Rules

- No integration may bypass safety or monetization doctrine.
- Cross‑pillar flows must be deterministic and documented.
- Dependencies must be reflected in `PLATFORM_DEPENDENCY_MAP`.
- Shared concepts (e.g., cosmetics, identity, tokens) must use canonical definitions from `TERMINOLOGY_AND_DEFINITIONS`.
