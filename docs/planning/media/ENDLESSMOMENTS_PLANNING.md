# ENDLESSMOMENTS_PLANNING

---

## 🎯 Purpose
Define the auto‑generated highlight clip system that captures gameplay moments and feeds EndlessTube and EndlessTV.

---

# 1. Goals
- Automatically capture gameplay highlights  
- Feed EndlessTube and EndlessTV  
- Provide shareable identity moments  
- Maintain gameplay‑only safety  

---

# 2. Personas
- **Player:** Wants cool clips  
- **Creator:** Wants highlight content  
- **Viewer:** Wants short, exciting moments  

---

# 3. User Stories
- *As a player, I want auto‑generated highlights.*  
- *As a creator, I want Moments to feed my Tube channel.*  
- *As a viewer, I want quick, exciting clips.*  

---

# 4. Core Capabilities
- Auto‑capture  
- Auto‑editing  
- Highlight tagging  
- Integration with Tube & TV  
- Brand‑safe overlays  

---

# 5. Workflows

### **5.1 Clip Generation**
1. Player triggers event (kill, win, milestone)  
2. System captures clip  
3. Clip is tagged and saved  
4. Clip appears in Moments feed  

---

# 6. Rules & Constraints
- Gameplay‑only  
- No external audio  
- No unsafe overlays  
- Must follow preset‑only rules  

---

# 7. Deep Dive

### **7.1 Highlight Logic**
- Kills  
- Wins  
- Clutch plays  
- Rank‑ups  
- Seasonal milestones  

### **7.2 Monetization**
- Ad inventory in highlight reels  
- Brand‑sponsored Moments  

---

# 8. Success Criteria
- High clip quality  
- Strong creator utility  
- High viewer engagement  
- Zero moderation required
