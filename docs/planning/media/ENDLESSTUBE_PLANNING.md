# ENDLESSTUBE_PLANNING

---

## 🎯 Purpose
Define the gameplay‑only VOD system that powers creator uploads, discovery, and monetization while maintaining zero‑moderation safety.

---

# 1. Goals
- Provide a safe, gameplay‑only video platform  
- Support creators with analytics and monetization  
- Feed EndlessTV and EndlessMoments  
- Maintain preset‑only interaction  

---

# 2. Personas
- **Player:** Watches gameplay videos  
- **Creator:** Uploads gameplay VODs  
- **Brand Partner:** Sponsors playlists or blocks  

---

# 3. User Stories
- *As a player, I want to watch gameplay‑only videos.*  
- *As a creator, I want to upload and monetize my gameplay.*  
- *As a brand, I want to sponsor playlists safely.*  

---

# 4. Core Capabilities
- Gameplay‑only uploads  
- Preset comments & reactions  
- Trending / Recommended feeds  
- Creator analytics  
- Monetization via ads  

---

# 5. Workflows

### **5.1 Upload Workflow**
1. Player records gameplay  
2. Uploads to EndlessTube  
3. System validates gameplay‑only content  
4. Video appears in feeds  

---

# 6. Rules & Constraints
- No external audio  
- No webcam or voice  
- Preset‑only comments  
- Ads must follow platform rules  

---

# 7. Deep Dive

### **7.1 Discovery**
- Trending  
- New  
- Recommended  
- Creator spotlights  

### **7.2 Monetization**
- Ad impressions → EndlessCoin  
- Brand‑sponsored playlists  
- Creator tiers unlock higher earnings  

---

# 8. Success Criteria
- Safe, zero‑moderation VOD  
- Strong creator ecosystem  
- High watch time  
- Reliable ad revenue flow
