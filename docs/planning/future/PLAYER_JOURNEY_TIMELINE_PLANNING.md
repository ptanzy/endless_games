# Player Journey Timeline Planning

This document covers the player journey timeline system, including data collection, visualization, and privacy considerations.

---

## 🎯 Purpose
Define the future Player Journey Timeline — a visual, chronological record of a player’s achievements, milestones, cosmetics, and seasonal participation across the entire EndlessGames ecosystem.

---

# 1. Goals
- Provide a meaningful long‑term identity timeline  
- Celebrate player achievements and milestones  
- Integrate with games, media, clans, and seasons  
- Maintain zero‑moderation safety  

---

# 2. Personas
- **Player:** Wants a visual record of their journey  
- **Creator:** Wants milestones surfaced  
- **Clan Member:** Wants clan achievements included  
- **Platform:** Wants a unified identity timeline  

---

# 3. User Stories
- *As a player, I want to see my progress over time.*  
- *As a creator, I want my milestones included.*  
- *As a clan member, I want clan events shown.*  

---

# 4. Core Capabilities
- Chronological timeline  
- Achievement markers  
- Seasonal participation badges  
- Cosmetic unlock history  
- Creator milestones  
- Clan event participation  

---

# 5. Workflows

### **5.1 Timeline Update Workflow**
1. Player completes milestone  
2. System logs event  
3. Timeline entry created  
4. Timeline updates visually  

---

# 6. Rules & Constraints
- No personal data  
- No unsafe content  
- No social messages  
- Only platform‑generated events allowed  

---

# 7. Deep Dive

### **7.1 Timeline Event Types**
- Level milestones  
- EndlessPass milestones  
- Prestige unlocks  
- Cosmetic unlocks  
- Clan achievements  
- Creator achievements  
- Seasonal participation  
- Media milestones  

### **7.2 Presentation**
- Vertical or horizontal timeline  
- Animated milestone cards  
- Seasonal color themes  
- Prestige‑tier visual upgrades  

---

# 8. Success Criteria
- Timeline feels meaningful and rewarding  
- Players see long‑term progress  
- Integrates cleanly with all systems  
- Zero moderation risk
