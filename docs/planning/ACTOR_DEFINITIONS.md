# ACTOR_DEFINITIONS

---

## 🎯 Purpose
Define all actors who interact with EndlessGames systems. These actors appear across planning documents and ensure consistent terminology, responsibilities, and system interactions across the entire platform.

---

# 1. Player Actors

### **Player**
The primary user of the platform. Engages with games, media, cosmetics, clans, events, and progression systems.

### **Prestige Player**
A long‑term player who has reached Level 100 and continues into infinite Prestige for cosmetic mastery.

### **Clan Member**
A player who participates in clan events, clan chat, clan treasury, and clan identity systems.

### **Creator**
A player who uploads gameplay‑only media, participates in creator tiers, earns creator rewards, and interacts with brand and monetization systems.

### **Brand Ambassador Player**
A player selected by brands (via platform mediation) to wear brand swag or appear in sponsored playlists.

---

# 2. Platform Actors

### **Platform**
The EndlessGames system itself. Owns identity, safety, progression, economy, media ingestion, and all enforcement of doctrine.

### **Internal Reviewer**
A platform‑authorized reviewer who approves:
- brand swag  
- preset packs  
- licensed cosmetics  
- creator tier upgrades  
- brand briefs  

No moderation of user content is required due to preset‑only and gameplay‑only rules.

### **System Administrator**
Internal operator responsible for:
- payouts  
- analytics  
- system health  
- observability  
- release management  
- internal dashboards  

---

# 3. Brand & Partner Actors

### **Brand Partner**
A company or organization that collaborates with EndlessGames through:
- brand swag  
- sponsored events  
- sponsored playlists  
- brand ambassador deals  
- EndlessRadio channels  

### **Brand Representative**
The individual at a partner brand who submits briefs, approves assets, and reviews campaigns.

---

# 4. Creator Ecosystem Actors

### **Creator Partner**
A creator who has reached Partner Tier and is eligible for:
- monetization  
- brand deals  
- revenue share  
- sponsored playlists  

### **Collaborating Creator**
A creator who participates in co‑authored playlists, Moments, or Tube series (future).

---

# 5. System Actors (Internal)

### **Economy Engine**
Automates token earnings, ad‑to‑cash, affiliate rewards, and clan treasury logic.

### **Media Engine**
Handles ingestion, processing, curation, and distribution of gameplay‑only media.

### **Preset Engine**
Manages preset messages, reactions, statuses, and preset‑safe content.

### **Cosmetic Engine**
Handles cosmetic inventory, layers, brand swag, prestige cosmetics, and unlock rules.

### **Progression Engine**
Manages XP, leveling, perks, prestige, and progression pacing.

---

# 6. Future Actors

### **Parent / Guardian**
For child accounts (future), manages permissions and safety settings.

### **Creator‑Developer**
A creator who builds games using the EndlessCreator SDK (far‑future).

---

# 7. Success Criteria
- All planning docs use consistent actor terminology  
- No ambiguous or overlapping actor definitions  
- Clear ownership and responsibilities across systems
