# PLATFORM_DOCTRINE_OVERVIEW

---

## 🎯 Purpose
Define the core doctrines that guide all EndlessGames systems, ensuring consistency, safety, and a unified player experience.

---

# 1. Identity Doctrine
- Identity is cosmetic‑only  
- Identity is cross‑surface  
- Identity is progression‑driven  
- Identity is seasonal  
- Identity must be safe and non‑abusive  

---

# 2. Safety Doctrine
- No open text  
- No unsafe communication  
- Preset‑only interactions  
- All content validated  
- Zero‑moderation by design  

---

# 3. Discovery Doctrine
- Personalized  
- Safe  
- Creator‑aware  
- Seasonal  
- Clan‑aware  
- Brand‑safe  

---

# 4. Creator Doctrine
- Creators are first‑class citizens  
- Creator tools unlock in tiers  
- Creator monetization is cosmetic‑only  
- Creator content must be safe  
- Creator identity is platform‑wide  

---

# 5. Seasonal Doctrine
- Seasons unify the platform  
- Seasonal identity is global  
- Seasonal content is time‑bound  
- Seasonal progression is predictable  
- Seasonal arcs integrate creators and clans  

---

# 6. Economy Doctrine
- No pay‑to‑win  
- Cosmetic‑only monetization  
- Predictable rewards  
- Controlled inflation  
- Player trust above all  

---

# 7. Enforcement
All systems must comply with these doctrines.  
No system may violate or override doctrine‑level rules.
