# LEVELING & PRESTIGE SYSTEM — PLANNING DOCUMENT

## Purpose
Define a long‑term progression system where players level from 1 to 100, earn Perk Points, and enter Prestige for cosmetic‑only rewards. The system must support multi‑year retention and integrate with quests, achievements, and events.

## Goals
- Provide a multi‑year progression arc (~5 years to Level 100).  
- Reward players with meaningful, non‑power perks.  
- Create a prestige layer that reinforces identity and long‑term engagement.  
- Maintain zero‑moderation and zero‑toxicity design.

## Personas
- **Long‑Term Player:** Wants a meaningful progression journey.  
- **Optimizer:** Wants perks that enhance earnings and discounts.  
- **Cosmetic Collector:** Wants prestige visuals.  
- **Competitive Player:** Wants to be first to reach milestones.

## User Stories
- *As a player, I want to level up so I feel long‑term progression.*  
- *As a player, I want perks that meaningfully enhance my experience.*  
- *As a prestige player, I want cosmetic rewards that show my dedication.*  
- *As the platform, I need a progression system that is safe and sustainable.*

## XP Curve
- Non‑linear curve.  
- Fast early levels, slow late levels.  
- Total XP target: 10–20 million XP for Level 100.  
- Designed for ~5 years of steady play.

## XP Sources
- Daily quests  
- Weekly quests  
- Achievements  
- Seasonal events  
- Gameplay  
- EndlessTube engagement  
- EndlessRadio listening  
- Offerwalls  

## Perk Points
Players earn **1 Perk Point per level** (100 total).  
Perk categories:
- Membership discounts  
- EndlessCoin boosts  
- Cash boosts  
- Ad reward boosts  
- XP boosts  
- Cosmetic unlocks  

## Prestige System
- Unlocks after Level 100.  
- Infinite Prestige Levels.  
- No additional Perk Points (avoid power creep).  
- Rewards:  
  - Prestige borders  
  - Prestige auras  
  - Prestige particle effects  
  - Prestige badges  
  - Prestige profile themes  

## First‑Player Legacy System
- First player to reach Level 100 in a season/year selects from preset options:  
  - Accent color  
  - Emblem  
  - Particle style  
  - Aura animation  
- Selection influences the next Prestige cosmetic set.  
- No user‑generated content.

## Workflows

### 1. Level Up
1. Player earns XP.  
2. System checks threshold.  
3. Level increases.  
4. Perk Point awarded.  
5. Perk tree available for allocation.

### 2. Prestige Advancement
1. Player reaches Level 100.  
2. Prestige unlocks.  
3. Prestige Level increments with XP.  
4. Prestige cosmetics awarded at milestones.

## Rules
- No gameplay power perks.  
- No user‑generated content.  
- No moderation required.  
- Perks must remain economically safe.

## Constraints
- Must avoid runaway XP inflation.  
- Must not create pay‑to‑win dynamics.  
- Must remain sustainable for multi‑year progression.

## Success Criteria
- High long‑term retention.  
- Strong engagement with quests and events.  
- High prestige participation.  
- Clear player understanding of perks and progression.
