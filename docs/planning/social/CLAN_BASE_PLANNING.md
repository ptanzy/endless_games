# CLAN_BASE_PLANNING

---

## 🎯 Purpose
Define the Clan Base — a cosmetic‑only shared space where clans display achievements, identity, and seasonal progress.

---

# 1. Goals
- Strengthen clan identity  
- Provide a shared cosmetic space  
- Support seasonal and brand integrations  
- Maintain zero‑moderation safety  

---

# 2. Personas
- **Clan Member:** Wants a shared identity space  
- **Clan Leader:** Wants a place to showcase achievements  
- **Brand Partner:** Wants safe clan‑based sponsorships  

---

# 3. User Stories
- *As a clan member, I want a shared space that represents our clan.*  
- *As a clan leader, I want to display achievements and cosmetics.*  
- *As a brand, I want to sponsor clan decorations safely.*  

---

# 4. Core Capabilities
- Cosmetic clan hall  
- Clan achievements display  
- Clan banners and badges  
- Seasonal decorations  
- Brand‑safe decorations (future)  

---

# 5. Workflows

### **5.1 Clan Base Update Workflow**
1. Clan earns achievements or cosmetics  
2. Clan leader applies decorations  
3. Clan Base updates instantly  
4. No unsafe content possible  

---

# 6. Rules & Constraints
- Cosmetic‑only  
- No open text  
- No user‑generated images  
- All decorations must be reviewed  

---

# 7. Deep Dive

### **7.1 Clan Base Features**
- Trophy wall  
- Seasonal decorations  
- Clan banners  
- Clan badges  
- Brand swag (future)  

### **7.2 Clan Economy Integration**
- Clan treasury (EndlessGold)  
- Clan event rewards  
- Clan ads (optional, preset‑safe)  

---

# 8. Success Criteria
- Strong clan identity  
- High clan engagement  
- Safe brand participation  
- Zero moderation required
