# PRESET_CREATOR_INTERACTIONS_PLANNING

---

## 🎯 Purpose
Define the preset‑only interaction system that allows players to safely engage with creators across EndlessTube, EndlessTV, EndlessStream, profiles, and events — without open text, unsafe messaging, or moderation requirements.

---

# 1. Goals

- Enable safe creator–viewer interaction  
- Support creator growth and engagement  
- Maintain zero‑moderation safety  
- Integrate with media, profiles, and events  
- Provide expressive preset tools for fans  

---

# 2. Personas

- **Viewer:** Wants to interact with creators safely  
- **Creator:** Wants engagement, feedback, and visibility  
- **Platform:** Needs a scalable, zero‑moderation creator ecosystem  

---

# 3. User Stories

- *As a viewer, I want to interact with creators using safe presets.*  
- *As a creator, I want preset‑only engagement on my videos and streams.*  
- *As a player, I want to follow creators and react to their content.*  
- *As the platform, I want creator interactions that require no moderation.*  

---

# 4. Core Capabilities

- Preset creator comments  
- Preset creator reactions  
- Creator‑specific preset packs  
- Creator milestone interactions  
- Creator follow system  
- Creator spotlight interactions  
- Safe creator–viewer conversation templates  

---

# 5. Workflows

### **5.1 Creator Interaction Workflow**
1. Viewer opens creator content (Tube, TV, Stream, Profile)  
2. Viewer selects a preset comment or reaction  
3. System validates preset  
4. Creator receives safe engagement  
5. No unsafe content or moderation required  

---

# 6. Rules & Constraints

- No open text  
- No voice or webcam  
- No personal data exchange  
- No user‑generated presets  
- All creator presets must be reviewed  
- All interactions must be gameplay‑only and safe for all ages  

---

# 7. Deep Dive

### **7.1 Interaction Surfaces**
- **EndlessTube:** Preset comments, reactions, creator badges  
- **EndlessTV:** Reactions, spotlight presets  
- **EndlessStream:** Preset chat, hype reactions, creator‑safe overlays  
- **Profiles:** Follow button, preset fan messages  
- **Events:** Creator‑led challenges with preset interactions  

---

### **7.2 Creator‑Specific Preset Packs**
Creators may unlock or earn:

- Creator‑branded reaction packs  
- Creator‑specific comment sets  
- Creator milestone presets (e.g., “Congrats on Partner!”)  
- Seasonal creator presets  

All must be reviewed and safe.

---

### **7.3 Creator Milestone Interactions**
Preset interactions appear when creators reach:

- New creator tier  
- Seasonal achievements  
- EndlessTube milestones  
- EndlessTV inclusion  
- Brand sponsorships (future)  

These interactions reinforce creator identity and viewer engagement.

---

### **7.4 Creator Safety Principles**
- No direct messaging  
- No custom text  
- No personal contact  
- No harassment vectors  
- No grooming vectors  
- All interactions are one‑to‑many or many‑to‑one, never private  

---

# 8. Success Criteria

- Creators receive high engagement  
- Viewers feel expressive and connected  
- No moderation incidents  
- Creator ecosystem grows safely  
- Preset interactions feel fun, expressive, and rewarding
