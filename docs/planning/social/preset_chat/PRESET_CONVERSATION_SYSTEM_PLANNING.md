# PRESET_CONVERSATION_SYSTEM_PLANNING

---

## 🎯 Purpose
Define the preset conversation system that enables structured, multi‑step interactions without open text or unsafe communication.

---

# 1. Goals
- Enable multi‑step preset conversations  
- Support social, clan, and creator interactions  
- Maintain zero‑moderation safety  
- Provide expressive, structured dialogue  

---

# 2. Personas
- **Player:** Wants lightweight conversations  
- **Clan Member:** Wants structured clan chat  
- **Creator:** Wants preset fan interactions  

---

# 3. User Stories
- *As a player, I want to have safe conversations using presets.*  
- *As a clan member, I want structured preset chat.*  
- *As a creator, I want preset fan interactions.*  

---

# 4. Core Capabilities
- Conversation templates  
- Branching preset options  
- Context‑aware responses  
- Multi‑step interactions  

---

# 5. Workflows

### **5.1 Conversation Workflow**
1. Player selects conversation starter  
2. System displays preset responses  
3. Conversation continues via branches  
4. No unsafe content possible  

---

# 6. Rules & Constraints
- No open text  
- No personal data exchange  
- No custom presets  
- All templates must be reviewed  

---

# 7. Deep Dive

### **7.1 Conversation Types**
- Social small talk  
- Clan coordination  
- Creator interactions  
- Event‑specific conversations  
- Seasonal conversations  

### **7.2 Future Enhancements**
- Cosmetic conversation packs  
- Creator‑authored preset packs (reviewed)  

---

# 8. Success Criteria
- Conversations feel natural  
- No moderation required  
- Players feel socially connected  
- Creators receive safe engagement
