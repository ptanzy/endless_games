# PRESET_REACTIONS_PLANNING

---

## 🎯 Purpose
Define the preset reaction system used across games, media, profiles, and clans to enable safe, expressive, zero‑moderation interaction.

---

# 1. Goals
- Provide expressive reactions  
- Maintain preset‑only safety  
- Support media and gameplay surfaces  
- Enable cosmetic monetization  

---

# 2. Personas
- **Player:** Wants expressive reactions  
- **Creator:** Wants engagement on videos  
- **Clan Member:** Wants reactions in clan chat  

---

# 3. User Stories
- *As a player, I want to react to videos safely.*  
- *As a creator, I want preset reactions on my content.*  
- *As a clan member, I want reactions in clan chat.*  

---

# 4. Core Capabilities
- Reaction packs  
- Cosmetic reaction unlocks  
- Reaction animations  
- Reaction usage tracking  

---

# 5. Workflows

### **5.1 Reaction Workflow**
1. Player selects reaction  
2. Reaction appears instantly  
3. Creator or player sees engagement  
4. No unsafe content possible  

---

# 6. Rules & Constraints
- No custom reactions  
- No text‑based reactions  
- All reactions must be preset and reviewed  
- Cosmetic reactions cannot affect gameplay  

---

# 7. Deep Dive

### **7.1 Reaction Packs**
- Seasonal packs  
- Prestige packs  
- Brand packs  
- Player‑made preset packs (reviewed)  

### **7.2 Surfaces**
- EndlessTube  
- EndlessTV  
- EndlessStream  
- Profiles  
- Clan chat  
- Game lobbies  

---

# 8. Success Criteria
- High reaction usage  
- Strong creator engagement  
- No moderation required  
- Cosmetic revenue opportunities
