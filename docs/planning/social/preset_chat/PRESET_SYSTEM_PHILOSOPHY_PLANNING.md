# PRESET_SYSTEM_PHILOSOPHY_PLANNING

---

## 🎯 Purpose
Define the philosophy behind EndlessGames’ preset‑only communication system, ensuring zero moderation, zero toxicity, and universal safety across all social surfaces.

---

# 1. Goals
- Eliminate all unsafe communication vectors  
- Maintain a fully zero‑moderation platform  
- Enable rich expression through safe presets  
- Support social, creator, and clan systems safely  

---

# 2. Personas
- **Player:** Wants safe, expressive communication  
- **Parent:** Wants a platform with no harassment risk  
- **Creator:** Wants preset‑safe engagement  
- **Platform:** Needs zero moderation overhead  

---

# 3. User Stories
- *As a player, I want to communicate safely without risk of harassment.*  
- *As a parent, I want a platform where my child cannot be contacted unsafely.*  
- *As a creator, I want preset‑only comments and reactions.*  
- *As the platform, I want no moderation burden.*  

---

# 4. Core Capabilities
- Preset comments  
- Preset reactions  
- Preset statuses  
- Preset clan chat  
- Preset creator interactions  
- Preset conversation templates  

---

# 5. Workflows

### **5.1 Preset Interaction Workflow**
1. Player selects preset  
2. System validates preset  
3. Preset is displayed  
4. No text entry or unsafe content possible  

---

# 6. Rules & Constraints
- No open text  
- No voice or webcam  
- No external audio  
- No user‑generated images  
- All presets must be reviewed and safe  

---

# 7. Deep Dive

### **7.1 Safety Principles**
- No harassment vectors  
- No grooming vectors  
- No personal data exchange  
- No moderation required  

### **7.2 Expression Principles**
- Presets must feel expressive  
- Presets must support social identity  
- Presets must support creators  
- Presets must support clans  

---

# 8. Success Criteria
- Zero moderation incidents  
- Players feel expressive  
- Parents trust the platform  
- Creators receive safe engagement
