# ENDLESSHUB_PLANNING

---

## 🎯 Purpose
Define the EndlessHub — the personalized, preset‑safe home feed that unifies gameplay, media, events, cosmetics, and social identity across the platform.

---

# 1. Goals
- Provide a dynamic, personalized home feed  
- Unify games, media, and social identity  
- Maintain zero‑moderation safety  
- Support creators, clans, and seasonal themes  

---

# 2. Personas
- **Player:** Wants a personalized, engaging home screen  
- **Creator:** Wants visibility in the hub  
- **Clan Member:** Wants clan activity surfaced  
- **Platform:** Wants a safe, curated discovery layer  

---

# 3. User Stories
- *As a player, I want a personalized feed of content and events.*  
- *As a creator, I want my videos and milestones surfaced.*  
- *As a clan member, I want clan activity highlighted.*  
- *As the platform, I want a safe, curated discovery experience.*  

---

# 4. Core Capabilities
- Personalized content feed  
- Game recommendations  
- Creator recommendations  
- Event and quest surfacing  
- Seasonal theme integration  
- Clan activity highlights  

---

# 5. Workflows

### **5.1 EndlessHub Personalization Workflow**
1. Player interacts with games/media  
2. System logs safe behavioral signals  
3. Hub ranks relevant content  
4. Feed updates dynamically  

---

# 6. Rules & Constraints
- No personal data  
- No open text  
- No unsafe social feeds  
- Only gameplay/media signals allowed  
- All content must be gameplay‑only  

---

# 7. Deep Dive

### **7.1 Hub Content Types**
- EndlessTube videos  
- EndlessTV highlights  
- EndlessMoments clips  
- Seasonal events  
- EndlessPass progress  
- Clan activity  
- Creator spotlights  
- Cosmetic recommendations  

### **7.2 Personalization Inputs**
- Gameplay history  
- Creator follows  
- Clan membership  
- Seasonal participation  
- Media watch history  

---

# 8. Success Criteria
- Feels alive and personalized  
- Drives cross‑platform engagement  
- Supports creators and clans  
- Zero moderation required
