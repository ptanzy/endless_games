# CLAN_SYSTEM_PLANNING

---

## 🎯 Purpose
Define the unified clan system for EndlessGames, including clan identity, clan chat, clan achievements, clan discovery, clan events, and clan integration across the Social & Identity Layer.

---

# 1. Goals
- Strengthen clan identity and belonging  
- Provide safe, preset‑only clan communication  
- Support clan achievements and progression  
- Improve clan discovery and visibility  
- Integrate clans across Hub, media, and profiles  
- Maintain zero‑moderation safety  

---

# 2. Personas

### **Clan Member**
Wants coordination, identity, and recognition.

### **Clan Leader**
Wants tools for organizing and motivating members.

### **Player**
Wants to discover clans that match their interests.

### **Platform**
Wants safe, scalable clan systems with no moderation.

---

# 3. User Stories

- *As a clan member, I want safe preset chat for coordination.*  
- *As a clan leader, I want clan achievements and progression.*  
- *As a player, I want to discover clans that match my interests.*  
- *As the platform, I want clan systems that require no moderation.*  

---

# 4. Core Capabilities

- Preset‑only clan chat  
- Clan identity surfaces  
- Clan achievements  
- Clan progression  
- Clan discovery  
- Clan events  
- Clan Hub modules  
- Clan profile integration  

---

# 5. Workflows

## **5.1 Clan Chat Workflow**
1. Player selects preset  
2. Message appears instantly  
3. Clan reacts using preset reactions  
4. No unsafe content possible  

---

## **5.2 Clan Achievement Workflow**
1. Clan performs social or gameplay action  
2. System logs safe signal  
3. Clan achievement unlocks  
4. Rewards distributed  

---

## **5.3 Clan Discovery Workflow**
1. System analyzes safe signals  
2. Clan recommendations generated  
3. Player sees suggestions in EndlessHub  
4. No personal data used  

---

# 6. Rules & Constraints

- No open text  
- No personal data  
- No unsafe communication  
- All clan presets reviewed  
- All clan identity assets reviewed  
- Clan discovery uses safe signals only  

---

# 7. Deep Dive

---

## **7.1 Clan Identity**

Clans have:

- Clan badge  
- Clan theme  
- Clan profile frame  
- Clan achievements  
- Clan seasonal identity  
- Clan event identity  

All identity assets must be preset‑safe.

---

## **7.2 Clan Achievements**

Categories include:

- Social participation  
- Clan events  
- Clan challenges  
- Media activity  
- Seasonal participation  

Rewards include:

- EndlessCoin  
- EndlessPass XP  
- Clan cosmetics  
- Clan badges  

---

## **7.3 Clan Discovery**

Uses safe signals:

- Shared gameplay  
- Shared creator follows  
- Shared seasonal participation  
- Shared media history  

No personal data is ever used.

---

## **7.4 Clan Integration Surfaces**

Clans appear in:

- EndlessHub  
- Profiles  
- EndlessTube  
- EndlessTV  
- EndlessStream  
- EndlessMoments  
- Social graph  
- Player expression system  

---

# 8. Success Criteria

- Strong clan identity  
- High clan engagement  
- Safe preset‑only communication  
- Better clan discovery  
- Zero moderation incidents
