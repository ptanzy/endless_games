# PRESET_COMMUNICATION_SYSTEM_PLANNING

---

## 🎯 Purpose
Define the unified preset‑only communication system used across EndlessGames. This system enables expressive, safe, zero‑moderation interaction across all social, creator, media, and clan surfaces. It consolidates comments, reactions, clan chat, statuses, creator interactions, conversation templates, and preset packs into one coherent communication engine.

---

# 1. Goals
- Provide expressive communication without open text  
- Maintain a fully zero‑moderation platform  
- Support social, creator, clan, and media interactions  
- Enable creator‑authored preset packs  
- Allow players to suggest presets externally  
- Ensure all presets follow tone, safety, and localization rules  
- Integrate seamlessly across all EndlessGames surfaces  

---

# 2. Personas

### **Player**
Wants expressive, safe communication across games, media, and clans.

### **Creator**
Wants preset‑safe engagement, branded preset packs, and fan interaction.

### **Clan Member**
Wants coordination and social bonding through preset chat.

### **Parent**
Wants a platform with no harassment or unsafe communication vectors.

### **Platform**
Needs a scalable, safe, zero‑moderation communication system.

---

# 3. User Stories

- *As a player, I want to communicate safely using expressive presets.*  
- *As a creator, I want preset‑only engagement on my content.*  
- *As a clan member, I want preset chat for coordination.*  
- *As a viewer, I want to react to videos and streams safely.*  
- *As a parent, I want a platform where my child cannot be contacted unsafely.*  
- *As the platform, I want communication that requires no moderation.*

# 4. Core Capabilities

- Preset comments  
- Preset reactions

- Preset creator interactions

- Creator‑authored preset packs  
- Seasonal and prestige preset packs

---

# 5. Workflows

## **5.1 Universal Preset Workflow**
1. Player selects preset  
2. System validates preset  
3. Preset is displayed instantly  
4. No unsafe content possible  

## **5.2 Creator Pack Workflow**
1. Creator designs preset pack using approved building blocks  
2. Creator submits pack for review  
3. Platform reviews for safety, tone, and localization  
4. Approved pack enters cosmetic catalog  
5. Players unlock or purchase the pack  

## **5.3 Player Suggestion Workflow**
1. Player suggests preset externally (Reddit, YouTube, Discord, future platform)  
2. Platform reviews suggestion  
3. Platform may convert suggestion into official preset  
4. Preset enters library after review  

---

# 6. Rules & Constraints

- No open text  
- No personal data exchange  
- No voice or webcam  
- No user‑generated images or audio  
- No custom emojis outside approved packs  
- All presets must be reviewed  
- All presets must follow tone, safety, and localization rules  
- All presets must be safe for all ages  
- Presets must never affect gameplay  
- Presets must never enable harassment or grooming vectors

## **7.7 Player‑Made Preset Packs**
Players cannot author packs directly but may suggest ideas externally.

### Capabilities
- Player suggestions via Reddit, YouTube, Discord, or future platform  
- Platform‑approved conversion into official presets  

### Rules
- No in‑platform submission  
- No user‑generated content  
- All packs must be platform‑authored or creator‑authored  

---

## **7.8 Creator‑Authored Preset Packs**
Creators can author preset packs using approved building blocks.

### Capabilities
- Reaction packs  
- Comment sets  
- Status sets  
- Milestone presets  
- Seasonal creator packs  

### Rules
- Must use platform‑approved assets  
- Must pass review  
- Can be monetized  
- Must follow tone, safety, and localization rules  

---

## **7.9 Preset Creation Roles**

### **Players**
- Cannot author preset packs  
- Cannot create custom presets  
- Cannot submit presets inside the platform  
- Can suggest presets externally  
- Can unlock and use preset packs  

### **Creators**
- Can author preset packs  
- Must use approved building blocks  
- Must pass review  
- Can monetize packs  

### **Platform**
- Owns preset doctrine  
- Owns preset engine  
- Reviews all presets  
- Publishes approved packs  
- Rejects unsafe or off‑tone content
