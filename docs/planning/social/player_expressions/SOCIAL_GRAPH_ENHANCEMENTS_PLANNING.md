# SOCIAL_GRAPH_ENHANCEMENTS_PLANNING

---

## 🎯 Purpose
Define enhancements to the social graph that improve discovery, relationships, and personalization while maintaining zero‑moderation safety.

---

# 1. Goals
- Improve social discovery  
- Strengthen player connections  
- Support creator and clan visibility  
- Maintain preset‑only safety  

---

# 2. Personas
- **Player:** Wants relevant social connections  
- **Creator:** Wants discoverability  
- **Clan Member:** Wants better clan visibility  

---

# 3. User Stories
- *As a player, I want to discover players with similar interests.*  
- *As a creator, I want my followers to find my content easily.*  
- *As a clan member, I want better clan discovery.*  

---

# 4. Core Capabilities
- Follow graph enhancements  
- Mutual interest signals  
- Personalized recommendations  
- Clan discovery improvements  

---

# 5. Workflows

### **5.1 Social Discovery Workflow**
1. System analyzes safe behavioral signals  
2. Recommendations generated  
3. Player sees suggestions in EndlessHub  
4. No unsafe data used  

---

# 6. Rules & Constraints
- No personal data  
- No open text  
- No unsafe social connections  
- Only gameplay/media signals allowed  

---

# 7. Deep Dive

### **7.1 Social Signals**
- Game history  
- Creator follows  
- Clan membership  
- Media watch history  
- Seasonal participation  

### **7.2 Recommendation Surfaces**
- EndlessHub  
- Creator pages  
- Clan discovery  
- Profile suggestions  

---

# 8. Success Criteria
- Better social discovery  
- Stronger creator visibility  
- Higher clan engagement  
- Zero moderation required
