# PLAYER_PRESET_PACKS_PLANNING

---

## 🎯 Purpose
Define the system that allows players (and creators) to use preset‑safe emote, reaction, and comment packs across the platform.

---

# 1. Goals
- Expand expressive preset options  
- Support cosmetic monetization  
- Maintain zero‑moderation safety  
- Enable creator‑made preset packs (reviewed)  

---

# 2. Personas
- **Player:** Wants expressive presets  
- **Creator:** Wants to design preset packs  
- **Platform:** Needs safe preset expansion  

---

# 3. User Stories
- *As a player, I want to unlock new preset packs.*  
- *As a creator, I want to design preset‑safe packs.*  
- *As the platform, I want presets that require no moderation.*  

---

# 4. Core Capabilities
- Reaction packs  
- Comment packs  
- Conversation packs  
- Seasonal preset packs  
- Creator‑made preset packs (reviewed)  

---

# 5. Workflows

### **5.1 Preset Pack Unlock Workflow**
1. Player unlocks preset pack  
2. Pack becomes available across surfaces  
3. Presets appear in menus  
4. No unsafe content possible  

---

# 6. Rules & Constraints
- No custom text  
- No user‑generated emojis  
- Creator packs must be reviewed  
- All presets must be safe for all ages  

---

# 7. Deep Dive

### **7.1 Pack Types**
- Seasonal  
- Prestige  
- Brand  
- Creator‑made  
- Event‑based  

### **7.2 Surfaces**
- EndlessTube  
- EndlessTV  
- EndlessStream  
- Clan chat  
- Profiles  
- EndlessHub  

---

# 8. Success Criteria
- High preset usage  
- Strong cosmetic revenue  
- Zero moderation required  
- Creators gain new expression tools
