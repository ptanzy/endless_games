# SOCIAL_ACHIEVEMENTS_PLANNING

---

## 🎯 Purpose
Define the social achievement system that rewards players for social engagement, clan participation, creator interaction, and media activity.

---

# 1. Goals
- Reward social participation  
- Strengthen clans and creators  
- Feed EndlessRank and EndlessPass  
- Maintain zero‑moderation safety  

---

# 2. Personas
- **Player:** Wants recognition for social activity  
- **Clan Member:** Wants clan‑related achievements  
- **Creator:** Wants achievements tied to engagement  

---

# 3. User Stories
- *As a player, I want achievements for social engagement.*  
- *As a clan member, I want clan achievements.*  
- *As a creator, I want achievements tied to my content.*  

---

# 4. Core Capabilities
- Social achievements  
- Clan achievements  
- Creator achievements  
- Media achievements  
- Seasonal achievements  

---

# 5. Workflows

### **5.1 Achievement Unlock Workflow**
1. Player performs social action  
2. System logs safe signal  
3. Achievement unlocks  
4. Rewards distributed  

---

# 6. Rules & Constraints
- No unsafe social actions  
- No open text  
- No personal data  
- All achievements must be preset‑safe  

---

# 7. Deep Dive

### **7.1 Achievement Categories**
- Social engagement  
- Clan participation  
- EndlessTube activity  
- EndlessTV inclusion  
- EndlessMoments usage  
- Creator milestones  

### **7.2 Rewards**
- EndlessCoin  
- EndlessPass XP  
- Badges  
- Cosmetics  

---

# 8. Success Criteria
- High social engagement  
- Strong clan participation  
- Creators feel rewarded  
- Zero moderation incidents
