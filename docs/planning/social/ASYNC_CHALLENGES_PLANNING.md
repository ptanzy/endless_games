# ASYNC_CHALLENGES_PLANNING

---

## 🎯 Purpose
Define asynchronous challenges that allow players to participate in tasks, quests, and competitions on their own schedule across all games and media.

---

# 1. Goals
- Support flexible, asynchronous participation  
- Drive cross‑game engagement  
- Integrate with EndlessPass and EndlessEvents  
- Maintain zero‑moderation safety  

---

# 2. Personas
- **Player:** Wants challenges they can complete anytime  
- **Creator:** Wants challenge‑driven visibility  
- **Clan Member:** Wants clan‑based async tasks  

---

# 3. User Stories
- *As a player, I want challenges I can complete on my own time.*  
- *As a creator, I want challenges that highlight my content.*  
- *As a clan member, I want clan async challenges.*  

---

# 4. Core Capabilities
- Daily/weekly/seasonal challenges  
- Cross‑game tasks  
- Media‑based tasks  
- Clan async challenges  
- Creator async challenges  

---

# 5. Workflows

### **5.1 Async Challenge Workflow**
1. Challenge appears in EndlessHub  
2. Player completes tasks across games/media  
3. System logs safe signals  
4. Rewards distributed  

---

# 6. Rules & Constraints
- No unsafe social actions  
- No open text  
- No competitive messaging  
- All tasks must be preset‑safe  

---

# 7. Deep Dive

### **7.1 Challenge Types**
- Gameplay tasks  
- Media tasks (Tube/TV/Moments)  
- Social tasks (preset‑safe)  
- Clan tasks  
- Creator‑led tasks  
- Seasonal tasks  

### **7.2 Rewards**
- EndlessCoin  
- EndlessPass XP  
- Cosmetics  
- Badges  

---

# 8. Success Criteria
- High participation  
- Strong cross‑game engagement  
- Supports creators and clans  
- Zero moderation required
