# ENDLESSCLAN — FULL FEATURE PLANNING DOCUMENT  
### (Comprehensive System Overview)

EndlessClan is the cross‑platform social identity utility of EndlessOS.  
It is not a content platform — it is a **persistent, deterministic, progression‑driven social layer** that enhances every other EndlessOS surface.

EndlessClan provides:
- group identity  
- group progression  
- group economy  
- group communication  
- group discovery  
- group incentives  

It is designed to be:
- safe  
- zero‑moderation  
- brand‑consistent  
- scalable  
- monetizable  
- cross‑platform  

This document outlines **every feature** of EndlessClan in full detail.

---

# 1. Purpose

EndlessClan exists to give players:
- a **home**  
- a **team**  
- a **shared identity**  
- a **shared economy**  
- a **shared progression path**  
- a **shared incentive loop**  

It enhances:
- EndlessGames (team play, quests, identity)  
- EndlessTube (creator promotion, clan videos)  
- EndlessTV (clan‑wide viewing incentives)  
- EndlessSocial (preset chat, reactions)  
- EndlessEvents (clan tournaments, clan weeks)  

EndlessClan is the **social backbone** of EndlessOS.

---

# 2. Core Concept

EndlessClan is a **utility**, not a platform.  
Users do not browse clans — they **belong** to one.

Clans provide:
- identity  
- communication  
- progression  
- treasury  
- quests  
- base  
- roles  
- public presence  
- monetization options  

Clans are cross‑platform and appear everywhere in EndlessOS.

---

# 3. Content Safety & Restrictions

EndlessClan is designed to be **zero‑moderation**.

### 3.1 No User‑Generated Text  
All text is:
- preset  
- safe  
- brand‑aligned  

### 3.2 No Voice Chat  
No audio communication.

### 3.3 No Image Uploads  
All clan emblems, banners, and badges are preset.

### 3.4 No External Links  
No URLs, no external media.

### 3.5 No Real‑Money Shared Pools  
Clan treasury cannot hold gift‑card cash.

### 3.6 No Toxicity  
Preset chat eliminates harassment, slurs, spam, grooming, etc.

This makes EndlessClan safe for all ages and all regions.

## 4.9 Clan XP From Player XP (Shared Progression System)

Clan XP is directly influenced by the activity of its members.  
Every time a player earns XP anywhere in EndlessOS — EndlessGames, EndlessTube, EndlessTV, EndlessEvents, or EndlessSocial — a portion of that XP is automatically converted into **Clan XP**, contributing to the clan’s overall level and progression.

This creates a powerful sense of shared growth: every action a member takes strengthens the clan.

### 4.9.1 Conversion Model
- **10% of all personal XP → Clan XP**  
- Awarded instantly  
- Reflected in the Clan XP bar  

### 4.9.2 XP Sources
- EndlessGames: matches, missions, achievements  
- EndlessTube: uploads, views, reactions  
- EndlessTV: viewing, creator features  
- EndlessEvents: milestones, participation  
- EndlessSocial: reactions, profile progression  

### 4.9.3 Clan XP Rewards
Clans unlock:
- banners  
- emblems  
- base decorations  
- cosmetics  
- treasury capacity  
- quest slots  
- event bonuses  
- dividend multipliers  
- leaderboard visibility  
- recruitment boosts  

### 4.9.4 Anti‑Abuse
- AFK detection  
- diminishing returns on repeated actions  
- daily XP caps  
- bot‑behavior filtering  

Clan XP becomes the backbone of long‑term clan progression.

# 7. Clan Ad Revenue Model

When a user views an ad on a clan‑related surface:

### Viewer receives:
- Coins  
- Gold  
- Gift‑card cash  

### Clan Members receive:
- Coins  
- Gold  
- Gift‑card cash  

### Clan Treasury receives:
- Coins  
- Gold  
- (NO cash)  

### Platform receives:
- Majority share  

This creates a **positive‑sum clan economy**.

---

# 8. Clan Dividend System

A portion of clan ad revenue is split evenly among all clan members.

### Dividend Types:
- Coins  
- Gold  
- Gift‑card cash  

### Rules:
- Dividends are automatic  
- Dividends are equal  
- Dividends are individual (not shared)  
- Dividends cannot be transferred  

This system encourages:
- clan loyalty  
- clan activity  
- clan recruitment  
- clan engagement  

---

# 9. Clan Activity Loop

1. User views ad  
2. User earns Coins/Gold/Cash  
3. Clan members earn Coins/Gold/Cash  
4. Clan treasury earns Coins/Gold  
5. Clan XP increases from player XP  
6. Clan levels up  
7. Clan unlocks new rewards  
8. Clan becomes more attractive  
9. More members join  
10. More ad views  
11. More XP  
12. Loop repeats  

This is a **self‑reinforcing ecosystem**.
