# OFFERWALL_SAFETY_FILTERS

---

## 🎯 Purpose

Define the safety, compliance, and content filtering rules that govern all offerwall vendors and offers integrated into EndlessGames.

---

# 1. Goals

- Protect players from unsafe offers  
- Maintain Zero Moderation  
- Ensure brand‑safe content  
- Comply with COPPA and global standards  

---

# 2. Prohibited Offer Types

- Gambling  
- Adult content  
- Unsafe crypto schemes  
- High‑risk financial products  
- Payday loans  
- Multi‑level marketing  
- Political surveys  
- Medical trials  

---

# 3. Required Disclosures

- Time estimate  
- Reward amount  
- Steps required  
- Partner identity  
- Cancellation rules (for trials)  

---

# 4. Safety Filters

### **4.1 Automated Filters**
- Keyword scanning  
- Category blocking  
- URL safety checks  
- Image safety scanning  

### **4.2 Manual Review**
- Human review of new offers  
- Brand safety checks  
- Seasonal review cycles  

### **4.3 COPPA Filtering**
- Remove all offers inappropriate for minors  
- No data‑collection offers for child accounts  

---

# 5. Vendor Requirements

- Must support offer blocking  
- Must support category filtering  
- Must support COPPA compliance  
- Must provide fraud prevention  

---

# 6. Deep Dive

### **6.1 Brand Safety**
All offers must be appropriate for all ages.

### **6.2 Zero Moderation**
No user‑generated content is allowed in any offer.

### **6.3 Fraud Prevention**
Vendors must provide:
- device fingerprinting  
- IP checks  
- anti‑bot systems  

---

# 7. Success Criteria

- No unsafe offers reach players  
- High trust in the offerwall  
- Strong compliance posture
