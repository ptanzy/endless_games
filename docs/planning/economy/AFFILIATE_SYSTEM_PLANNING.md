# AFFILIATE_SYSTEM_PLANNING

---

## 🎯 Purpose

Define the affiliate system (EndlessDeals) that rewards players and creators for completing partner offers, generating impressions, and driving purchases — safely and transparently.

---

# 1. Goals

- Provide a safe, transparent affiliate hub  
- Reward players for impressions and purchases  
- Enable creator affiliate revenue  
- Maintain strict safety and disclosure rules  

---

# 2. Personas

- **Player:** Wants to earn EndlessCoin  
- **Creator:** Wants affiliate revenue  
- **Affiliate Partner:** Wants conversions  
- **Platform Operator:** Needs deterministic rules  

---

# 3. User Stories

- *As a player, I want to complete offers for EndlessCoin.*  
- *As a creator, I want affiliate revenue from my content.*  
- *As a partner, I want clear attribution.*  

---

# 4. Core Capabilities

- Offerwall integration  
- Impression tracking  
- Conversion tracking  
- Creator attribution  
- Reward distribution  
- Safety validation  

---

# 5. Workflows

### **5.1 Player Offer Completion**
1. Player selects offer  
2. Completes task  
3. Partner confirms  
4. Player receives EndlessCoin  

### **5.2 Creator Affiliate Attribution**
1. Viewer clicks offer from creator content  
2. Conversion tracked  
3. Creator receives share  

---

# 6. Rules & Constraints

- No predatory offers  
- Clear time/value disclosure  
- No dark patterns  
- No unsafe external content  

---

# 7. Deep Dive

### **7.1 Offer Types**
- Surveys  
- App installs  
- Purchases  
- Trials  
- Time‑based tasks  

### **7.2 Creator Attribution**
- Link tracking  
- Playlist‑based attribution  
- EndlessTube/TV integration  

### **7.3 Safety**
- All offers manually reviewed  
- No adult content  
- No gambling  
- No crypto scams  

---

# 8. Success Criteria

- Players trust the system  
- Creators earn fairly  
- Partners see reliable conversions
