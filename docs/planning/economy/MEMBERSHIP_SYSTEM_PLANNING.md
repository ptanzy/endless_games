# MEMBERSHIP SYSTEM — PLANNING DOCUMENT

## Purpose
Define a player‑first membership system that provides Ad Controls, bonuses, cosmetic value, and progression enhancements without introducing pay‑to‑win mechanics or removing the ability to earn from ads.

## Goals
- Increase membership conversions through meaningful, non‑exploitative value.
- Provide players with control over their ad experience.
- Integrate cleanly with economy, leveling, and seasonal systems.
- Maintain zero‑moderation and zero‑toxicity design.

## Personas
- **Value Seeker:** Wants boosts and discounts.  
- **Ad‑Sensitive Player:** Wants to disable specific ad types.  
- **Grinder:** Wants XP and token bonuses.  
- **Creator:** Wants analytics and publishing tools.

## User Stories
- *As a player, I want to choose which ads I see so I can control my experience.*  
- *As a member, I want bonuses that make progression feel smoother.*  
- *As a creator, I want tools that help me grow.*  
- *As the platform, I need a membership system that is safe and non‑exploitative.*

## Membership Tiers
- **Basic Membership:**  
  - Ad Controls  
  - Small XP and token boosts  
  - Profile themes  

- **Premium Membership:**  
  - Premium EndlessPass  
  - Premium cosmetics  
  - Enhanced Moments features  

- **Creator Membership:**  
  - Analytics  
  - Templates  
  - Scheduling tools  

## Core Capabilities
- Ad Controls  
- Token bonuses  
- Cash bonuses  
- XP bonuses  
- Cosmetic unlocks  
- Seasonal discount integration  
- Perk‑based discount integration  

## Ad Controls
Members may toggle:
- Banner ads  
- EndlessTube ads  
- EndlessRadio ads  
- Creator ads  
- Offerwall notifications  

Disabling an ad type disables rewards from that surface.

## Workflows

### 1. Membership Activation
1. Player purchases membership.  
2. System applies tier benefits.  
3. Ad Controls unlock.  
4. Bonuses activate.  
5. Membership badge appears.

### 2. Ad Control Toggle
1. Player toggles ad type.  
2. System updates eligibility.  
3. Reward surfaces adjust.  
4. Bonuses apply only to enabled surfaces.

## Rules
- No pay‑to‑win benefits.  
- No gameplay power.  
- No forced ad removal.  
- Bonuses must remain within safe economic bounds.

## Constraints
- Must remain sustainable.  
- Must not reduce ad revenue below safe thresholds.  
- Must not create confusion around disabled ad rewards.

## Success Criteria
- Increased membership conversions.  
- High retention among members.  
- Clear understanding of Ad Controls.  
- No negative impact on ad ecosystem.
