# Token Earn Cap Planning

---

## 🎯 Purpose
Define the cap philosophy, tiered earn structure, and anti‑abuse protections governing EndlessCoin and EndlessGold earnings across all surfaces. Caps are designed to prevent botting, fraud, and unhealthy farming — not to restrict normal or dedicated players.

---

# 1. Goals
- Protect the economy from abuse and automation  
- Ensure caps reflect reasonable human behavior  
- Maintain fairness between casual and dedicated players  
- Preserve long‑term economic stability  
- Support healthy play patterns  
- Integrate with all earn surfaces (ads, affiliate, gameplay, social, clan, seasonal, prestige)  

---

# 2. Personas
- **Player:** Wants to earn freely without feeling restricted  
- **Dedicated Player:** Wants to play a lot without hitting walls  
- **Platform:** Needs to prevent abuse and maintain stability  
- **Economy Designer:** Needs predictable currency flow  

---

# 3. Philosophy: Caps Should Reflect Reasonable Behavior
Caps are not meant to limit players — they are meant to limit **unreasonable** behavior.

### Caps should:
- Never hinder normal players  
- Rarely affect dedicated players  
- Always stop bots, exploiters, and unhealthy farming  
- Protect the value of EndlessCoin  
- Maintain pacing across seasons and systems  

### Caps should NOT:
- Create pressure  
- Force daily check‑ins  
- Punish high engagement  
- Feel like “walls”  

The goal is **invisible guardrails**, not restrictions.

---

# 4. Cap Types

### **Hard Cap**
A strict limit.  
Used only where abuse is extremely likely (ads, affiliate).

### **Soft Cap**
Earn rate slows down after a threshold.  
Used for gameplay and clan earn.

### **Micro Cap**
Very small limit.  
Used for social earn to prevent spam.

### **No Cap**
Used for long‑term progression (seasonal, prestige).

# 6. Bot‑Resistance Model (Per Earn Source)

### **6.1 Rewarded Ads**
- **Bot Risk:** Extremely high  
- **Human Behavior:** 3–10/day  
- **Bot Behavior:** 200–500/day  
- **Defense:** Hard cap + cooldowns  
- **Outcome:** Real players unaffected; bots eliminated  

---

### **6.2 Offerwall**
- **Bot Risk:** High  
- **Human Behavior:** 1–3/week  
- **Bot Behavior:** 20–100/day  
- **Defense:** Soft cap + fraud scoring  
- **Outcome:** Real players rewarded; fraud prevented  

---

### **6.3 Affiliate Actions**
- **Bot Risk:** Very high (real money involved)  
- **Human Behavior:** 1–3/day  
- **Bot Behavior:** 50–200/day  
- **Defense:** Hard cap + per‑campaign limits  
- **Outcome:** Protects advertisers + platform  

---

### **6.4 Gameplay Earn**
- **Bot Risk:** Medium  
- **Human Behavior:** 200–800 EC/day  
- **Dedicated Player:** 800–1200 EC/day  
- **Bot Behavior:** 10,000+ EC/day  
- **Defense:** Soft cap (slowdown, not stop)  
- **Outcome:** Healthy pacing; bots lose efficiency  

---

### **6.5 Social Earn**
- **Bot Risk:** Extremely high  
- **Human Behavior:** 10–30/day  
- **Bot Behavior:** 500–2000/day  
- **Defense:** Micro cap + anti‑spam rules  
- **Outcome:** Social surfaces stay healthy  

---

### **6.6 Clan Earn**
- **Bot Risk:** Medium  
- **Human Behavior:** 100–300 EC/day  
- **Bot Behavior:** 2000+/day  
- **Defense:** Soft cap + activity checks  
- **Outcome:** Encourages teamwork without farming  

---

### **6.7 Seasonal Earn**
- **Bot Risk:** Low  
- **Human Behavior:** Variable  
- **Defense:** No cap needed  
- **Outcome:** Long‑term progression stays meaningful  

---

### **6.8 Prestige Earn**
- **Bot Risk:** Very low  
- **Human Behavior:** Slow, mastery‑based  
- **Defense:** No cap needed  
- **Outcome:** Prestige remains exclusive and earned  

---

# 7. Rules & Constraints
- Caps must never punish normal players  
- Caps must always stop bots and exploiters  
- Caps must be invisible to 95% of players  
- Soft caps must never reduce earn to zero  
- Hard caps only used where absolutely necessary  
- All earn events logged for audit and tuning  

---

# 8. Success Criteria
- Players feel rewarded, not restricted  
- Bots and exploiters are neutralized  
- Economy remains stable long‑term  
- Earn pacing supports seasonal and prestige systems  
- Free players remain competitive  
- No burnout or pressure loops
