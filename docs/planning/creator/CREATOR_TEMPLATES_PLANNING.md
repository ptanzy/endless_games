# CREATOR_TEMPLATES_PLANNING

---

## 🎯 Purpose

Creator Templates provide preset overlays, highlight formats, and visual styles that creators can apply to EndlessTube videos.

---

# 1. Goals

- Improve creator production quality  
- Provide safe preset‑only visual styles  
- Support brand‑sponsored templates  
- Maintain gameplay‑only rules  

---

# 2. Personas

- **Creator:** Wants better visuals  
- **Viewer:** Wants polished content  
- **Brand Partner:** Wants branded templates  

---

# 3. User Stories

- *As a creator, I want to apply a preset overlay to my video.*  
- *As a creator, I want seasonal templates.*  
- *As a brand, I want branded templates.*  

---

# 4. Core Capabilities

- Preset overlays  
- Highlight formats  
- Seasonal templates  
- Brand templates  

---

# 5. Workflows

### **5.1 Applying a Template**
1. Creator selects template  
2. System applies overlay  
3. Creator previews  
4. Creator publishes  

---

# 6. Rules & Constraints

- No custom text  
- No external images  
- No unsafe symbols  
- All templates must be preset  

---

# 7. Deep Dive

### **7.1 Template Types**
- Seasonal  
- Game‑specific  
- Brand‑sponsored  
- Creator milestone templates  

### **7.2 Template Engine**
- Deterministic rendering  
- Safe metadata  
- Thumbnail generation  

---

# 8. Success Criteria

- Creators produce higher‑quality content  
- Templates remain safe  
- Brands gain new integration surfaces
