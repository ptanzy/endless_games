# CREATOR_COLLAB_PLANNING

---

## 🎯 Purpose

Creator Collaboration enables two or more creators to co‑author EndlessTube playlists, EndlessMoments highlight reels, and future collaborative content formats.  
It expands the creator ecosystem by supporting shared creativity while maintaining Zero Moderation safety.

---

# 1. Goals

- Enable creators to collaborate safely without open communication  
- Support co‑authored playlists, series, and highlight reels  
- Expand creator discovery through shared audiences  
- Provide clear ownership, attribution, and revenue rules  
- Maintain deterministic, preset‑only workflows

---

# 2. Personas

- **Creator A (Primary):** Wants to collaborate with another creator to grow reach  
- **Creator B (Co‑Author):** Wants shared visibility and shared playlists  
- **Viewer:** Wants curated, high‑quality collaborative content  
- **Brand Partner (Future):** Wants to sponsor co‑authored content blocks  

---

# 3. User Stories

- *As a creator, I want to invite another creator to co‑author a playlist so we can grow together.*  
- *As a creator, I want to approve or decline collaboration requests safely.*  
- *As a creator, I want shared analytics so I can see how our collaboration performs.*  
- *As a viewer, I want to discover collaborative playlists from creators I follow.*  
- *As a brand partner, I want to sponsor co‑authored playlists for seasonal campaigns.*  

---

# 4. Core Capabilities

- Collaboration invitations (preset‑only)  
- Co‑authored EndlessTube playlists  
- Co‑authored EndlessMoments highlight reels  
- Shared analytics  
- Shared monetization rules  
- Ownership and attribution metadata  
- Safe preset‑only communication between collaborators  

---

# 5. Workflows

### **5.1 Collaboration Request**
1. Creator A selects “Invite Collaborator”  
2. Creator B receives preset‑only invitation  
3. Creator B accepts or declines  
4. Collaboration space is created  

### **5.2 Co‑Authoring Content**
1. Creators add videos or Moments  
2. System validates content safety  
3. Playlist or reel updates automatically  
4. Both creators appear as co‑authors  

### **5.3 Publishing**
1. Co‑authored content enters staging  
2. Both creators approve  
3. Content goes live  

---

# 6. Rules & Constraints

- No open text or chat between creators  
- All communication is preset‑only  
- All content must be gameplay‑only  
- No external audio or commentary  
- Co‑authored monetization follows platform rules  
- Ownership metadata must be deterministic  

---

# 7. Deep Dive

### **7.1 Co‑Authored Playlists**
- Auto‑updating playlists based on tags, games, or series  
- Shared visibility across both creator profiles  
- Shared EndlessTube discovery ranking  

### **7.2 Co‑Authored Moments**
- Combined highlight reels  
- Auto‑curated based on gameplay events  
- Seasonal or event‑based collaborative reels  

### **7.3 Monetization**
- Shared ad revenue  
- Shared affiliate revenue  
- Shared brand sponsorship revenue (future)  

---

# 8. Success Criteria

- Creators collaborate safely without open communication  
- Co‑authored content increases discovery  
- Monetization is fair and transparent  
- Zero Moderation rules remain intact
