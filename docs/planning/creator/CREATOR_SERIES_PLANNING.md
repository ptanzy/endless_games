# CREATOR_SERIES_PLANNING

---

## 🎯 Purpose

Creator Series allows creators to group related videos into auto‑updating long‑form content arcs.

---

# 1. Goals

- Support episodic content  
- Auto‑update series based on tags  
- Improve viewer retention  
- Expand creator storytelling  

---

# 2. Personas

- **Creator:** Wants to build a series  
- **Viewer:** Wants bingeable content  
- **Brand Partner:** Wants to sponsor series  

---

# 3. User Stories

- *As a creator, I want to group my videos into a series.*  
- *As a viewer, I want to watch a series in order.*  
- *As a creator, I want new episodes to auto‑add.*  

---

# 4. Core Capabilities

- Series creation  
- Auto‑updating playlists  
- Episode ordering  
- Series metadata  

---

# 5. Workflows

### **5.1 Creating a Series**
1. Creator selects videos  
2. Adds metadata  
3. System auto‑orders episodes  

---

# 6. Rules & Constraints

- Gameplay‑only content  
- No external audio  
- No unsafe metadata  

---

# 7. Deep Dive

### **7.1 Auto‑Updating**
- Tag‑based  
- Game‑based  
- Event‑based  

### **7.2 Discovery**
- Series pages  
- Series badges  
- Series recommendations  

---

# 8. Success Criteria

- Creators build long‑form arcs  
- Viewers engage more deeply  
- Series remain safe and deterministic
