# Brand Swag Production Pipeline

---

## 🎯 Purpose
Define the end‑to‑end pipeline for producing, approving, manufacturing, and distributing EndlessGames swag.

---

# 1. Goals
- Ensure consistent quality across all swag
- Maintain predictable production timelines
- Support limited runs and mass production
- Provide transparency from concept to delivery

---

# 2. Personas
- **Designer:** Creates swag concepts
- **Production Manager:** Oversees manufacturing
- **Brand Manager:** Approves final designs
- **Partner/Vendor:** Manufactures swag

---

# 3. User Stories
- *As a production manager, I want clear timelines and specs.*
- *As a designer, I want predictable approval workflows.*

---

# 4. Core Capabilities
- Design → Approval → Manufacturing → QA → Distribution
- Vendor management
- Material specifications
- Production tracking
- Quality assurance

---

# 5. Workflows

### **5.1 Pre‑Production**
1. Concept creation  
2. Material selection  
3. Costing  
4. Prototype creation  
5. Approval  

### **5.2 Production**
1. Manufacturing  
2. QA checks  
3. Packaging  

### **5.3 Distribution**
1. Inventory allocation  
2. Shipping  
3. Event or ambassador distribution  

---

# 6. Rules & Constraints
- All vendors must meet quality standards  
- No unapproved materials  
- Production timelines must be tracked  

---

# 7. Deep Dive
- Vendor tiers  
- QA scoring  
- Limited‑run production rules  

---

# 8. Success Criteria
- On‑time production  
- Low defect rate  
- High partner satisfaction
