# BRAND_EVENTS_PLANNING

---

## 🎯 Purpose
Define how brands can safely sponsor events, playlists, cosmetics, and seasonal content across the EndlessGames ecosystem.

---

# 1. Goals
- Enable brand‑safe sponsorships  
- Support seasonal brand events  
- Maintain zero‑moderation safety  
- Provide value to players and creators  

---

# 2. Personas
- **Brand Partner:** Wants safe, high‑visibility placements  
- **Player:** Wants fun, rewarding events  
- **Creator:** Wants brand‑boosted exposure  

---

# 3. User Stories
- *As a brand, I want to sponsor seasonal events safely.*  
- *As a player, I want rewards from brand events.*  
- *As a creator, I want brand‑boosted visibility.*  

---

# 4. Core Capabilities
- Brand‑sponsored events  
- Brand cosmetics  
- Brand playlists (Tube/TV)  
- Brand clan tournaments  
- Sponsored EndlessRadio channels  

---

# 5. Workflows

### **5.1 Brand Event Workflow**
1. Brand submits brief  
2. EndlessGames designs event + cosmetics  
3. Brand approves  
4. Event launches  
5. Rewards distributed  

---

# 6. Rules & Constraints
- Cosmetic‑only brand items  
- No unsafe brand messaging  
- No direct brand‑to‑player communication  
- All content must be gameplay‑only  

---

# 7. Deep Dive

### **7.1 Brand Surfaces**
- EndlessTube playlists  
- EndlessTV blocks  
- EndlessRadio channels  
- Seasonal events  
- Cosmetics  
- Clan tournaments  

### **7.2 Brand Swag Pipeline**
- Concept → Review → Approval → Implementation  

---

# 8. Success Criteria
- Brands trust the platform  
- Players enjoy brand events  
- Creators gain exposure  
- Zero moderation required
