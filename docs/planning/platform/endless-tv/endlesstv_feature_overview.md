# EndlessTV — Full Platform Feature Overview (Detailed Planning Document)

## 1. Purpose
EndlessTV is a 24/7, always‑on broadcast channel powered entirely by **EndlessTube gameplay videos** created inside the EndlessOS ecosystem.  
It is a lean‑back, passive viewing experience that blends:

- creator‑driven promotion  
- automated programming  
- safe, brand‑consistent content  
- ad‑supported monetization  
- cross‑platform discovery  

EndlessTV is not a traditional streaming service.  
It is a **creator‑powered broadcast network** built on top of EndlessGames and EndlessTube.

---

# 2. Core Concept
EndlessTV is a **continuous autoplay channel** that plays gameplay videos submitted by creators.  
Creators pay **EndlessCoins** or **EndlessGold** to place their videos into the EndlessTV queue.  
Before each video, a short ad plays.  
Creators earn a share of the ad revenue in:

- EndlessCoins  
- EndlessGold  
- Gift‑card cash (small percentage)  

This creates a **closed, self‑reinforcing creator economy**.

---

# 3. Content Restrictions (Critical to Platform Identity)

EndlessTV only accepts videos that meet all of the following:

### 3.1 Created Inside EndlessOS
- Only videos recorded using the EndlessGames recording system.
- No external uploads.
- No external editing tools.

### 3.2 Gameplay‑Only Content
- Must be actual gameplay from EndlessGames.
- No webcam footage.
- No commentary.
- No overlays.

### 3.3 Audio Restrictions
- Only game audio OR  
- Music from EndlessMusic (licensed internally).

### 3.4 No External Media
- No copyrighted music.
- No voiceovers.
- No external sound effects.
- No third‑party assets.

### 3.5 Automatic Safety
Because of these restrictions:
- No moderation required.
- No legal risk.
- No content unpredictability.
- No brand‑unsafe material.

This is a **closed, safe, deterministic content pipeline**.

# 5. Creator Promotion System

## 5.1 Submission Flow
Creators choose:
- which EndlessTube gameplay video to promote  
- how many times it should appear  
- which currency to pay with (Coins or Gold)  

## 5.2 Pricing Model (Length‑Based)
Longer videos cost more because they occupy more airtime.

Example:
- 1–3 min → 50 Coins / 1 Gold  
- 3–10 min → 150 Coins / 3 Gold  
- 10–20 min → 300 Coins / 5 Gold  
- 20+ min → 500 Coins / 8 Gold  

## 5.3 Queue Placement
Queue is:
- chronological  
- weighted by payment amount  
- capped to prevent monopolization  
- filtered to avoid back‑to‑back repeats  

## 5.4 Priority Slots (Optional)
Creators can pay extra for:
- peak hours  
- featured blocks  
- “prime time” slots  
- category‑specific blocks  

---

# 6. Monetization Model

## 6.1 Pre‑Roll Ads
A short ad plays before every EndlessTV video.

## 6.2 Mid‑Roll Ads (Optional)
For videos longer than 10 minutes.

## 6.3 Revenue Split (Example)
For each ad impression:
- 50% → EndlessOS  
- 30% → Creator in EndlessCoins  
- 15% → Creator in EndlessGold  
- 5% → Creator in gift‑card cash  

## 6.4 Why This Works
- EndlessOS earns revenue.  
- Creators get paid for exposure.  
- Creators reinvest earnings into more promotion.  
- The ecosystem becomes self‑sustaining.

# 8. Queue System (Detailed)

## 8.1 Queue Structure
The queue is a rolling list of:
- creator‑submitted videos  
- EndlessOS filler content (optional)  
- sponsored blocks (optional)  

## 8.2 Queue Rules
- No creator can dominate the queue.  
- No video can appear twice in a row.  
- No more than X videos per creator per hour.  
- Queue refreshes every N minutes.  

## 8.3 Queue Weighting
Factors:
- payment amount  
- video length  
- creator priority tier  
- time of submission  

## 8.4 Queue Transparency
Creators see:
- exact position  
- estimated play time  
- competition level  
- recommended bid

# 9. Anti‑Abuse & Fairness Systems

## 9.1 Anti‑Farm Rules
Creators cannot:
- watch their own EndlessTV slots  
- artificially inflate impressions  
- loop their own videos  

## 9.2 Quality Requirements
Videos must:
- meet minimum resolution  
- meet minimum length  
- contain actual gameplay  

## 9.3 Cooldowns
Creators can only submit:
- X videos per day  
- Y minutes per week  

## 9.4 Duplicate Prevention
Same video cannot appear:
- twice in the same hour  
- more than N times per day

# 14. Discovery & Engagement Systems (Ensuring EndlessTV Is Actively Used)

EndlessTV is not designed to be a destination users must remember to open.

The Discovery System ensures EndlessTV remains visible, relevant, and continuously engaged without relying on notifications or intrusive prompts.
